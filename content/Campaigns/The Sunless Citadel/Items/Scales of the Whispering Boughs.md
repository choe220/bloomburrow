# Scales of the Whispering Boughs

*Wondrous item, uncommon*

---

This delicate scale is made from the two halves of a perfectly split milkweed pod, suspended by strands of glistening spider silk from a tiny, silver-barked twig. Faint etchings of sprouting seeds adorn one pod-pan, while images of withered leaves adorn the other.

You can use the scale to cast *detect evil and good* as a ritual. To do so, you must place the scale on a solid surface and sprinkle the pans with fresh morning dew or place a clear quartz pebble in each pan.

The scale remains motionless if it detects nothing. It tips toward the sprouting seed pan if it detects a celestial, or a place strongly tied to natural purity (consecrated). It tips toward the withered leaf pan if it detects an aberration, fiend, or undead, or a place tainted by blight (desecrated). It fluctuates slightly if it detects a fey or elemental. By touching the scales after casting the ritual, you instantly learn any information the spell can normally convey, and then the effect ends.
