*Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement)*

---

This dark cloak is made of the cured hide of a great wolf, its fur coarse and patchy, with small, thorn-like protrusions growing from the leather.

As an action, you can command the cloak to transform you into a **Blighted Wolf** for up to 1 hour. A Blighted Wolf uses the **Worg** stat block, but its bite deals an additional 1d6 poison damage. The transformation otherwise functions as the *polymorph* spell, but you can use a bonus action to revert to your normal form.

***Curse.*** This cloak is cursed with the essence of a creature twisted by blight, and becoming attuned to it extends the curse to you. Until the curse is broken with *remove curse* or similar magic, you are unwilling to part with the cloak, keeping it within reach at all times.

The sixth time you use the cloak, and each time thereafter, you must make a DC 15 Charisma saving throw. On a failed save, the transformation lasts until dispelled or until you drop to 0 hit points, and you can't willingly return to normal form. If you ever remain in Blighted Wolf form for 6 hours, the transformation becomes permanent and you lose your sense of self. All your statistics are then replaced by those of a Blighted Wolf. Thereafter, only *remove curse* or similar magic allows you to regain your identity and return to normal. If you remain in this permanent form for 6 days, only a *wish* spell can reverse the transformation.
