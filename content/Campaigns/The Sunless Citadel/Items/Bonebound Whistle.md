*Wondrous item, uncommon*

---

This whistle is carved from transparent crystal, and it resembles a tiny, skeletal animal curled up. Lore recalled with a successful DC 20 Intelligence (Arcana or History) check suggests that the ancient inhabitants of this land created these whistles to command the spirits of their dead.

If you blow the whistle in darkness or under the night sky, it allows you to cast the *animate dead* spell, but you may only target the bones of animalfolk. The created creature is a **Bonebound** (use the appropriate undead stat block, e.g., Skeleton or Zombie). The target can be affected through up to 10 feet of soft earth, and if it is, it takes 1 minute to claw its way to the surface to serve you.

Once the whistle has animated an undead creature, it can't do so again until 7 days have passed. Once every 24 hours, you can blow the whistle to reassert control over one or two creatures you animated with it.
