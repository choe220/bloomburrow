# Magic Items of the Sunless Citadel

The Sunless Citadel, a place of shadow and blight, holds secrets and treasures for those brave enough to explore its depths. The items listed below are some of the magical artifacts that can be discovered within its crumbling walls and twisted gardens.

Many of these items are imbued with the Citadel's corrupted magic or the ancient power of the druids who once dwelled there. Adventurers should be wary, as not all that glitters is gold, and some of these powerful items carry dangerous curses.

This list provides a brief overview of each item. Click on an item's name to learn more about its properties, powers, and potential dangers.

| Item                                 | Description |
| ------------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [[Amulet of the Uncorrupted Spirit]] | Grants advantage on saving throws against effects from blighted or undead creatures. |
| [[Bonebound Whistle]]                | Animates the bones of animalfolk as undead servants. |
| [[Bracelet of Petrified Bark]]       | Provides immunity to petrification and can turn others to stone, but is cursed. |
| [[Burdensome Geode]]                 | A cursed geode that magically encumbers its carrier. |
| [[Cloak of the Blighted Hunter]]     | A cursed cloak that allows the wearer to transform into a Blighted Wolf. |
| [[Mantle of the Sun-Warmed Field]]   | A warm mantle that grants resistance to cold and extreme temperatures. |
| [[Murky River-Stone]]                | A cursed stone that appears to be a *stone of good luck* but instead gives a penalty. |
| [[Pollen of Servitude]]              | A magical pollen that can dominate a creature of a specific type. |
| [[Pool of Ancient Memories]]         | A bowl of water that reveals cryptic visions of a target's past. |
| [[Root-Cleaver]]                     | A +1 wooden longsword that is especially effective against plant and earth creatures. |
| [[Scales of the Whispering Boughs]]  | A magical scale that can detect good, evil, and other spiritual forces. |
| [[Thistle-Sting]]                    | A sentient +1 rapier effective against large monsters, with the power of a *wand of enemy detection*. |
| [[Thorn-Vine Spear]]                 | A cursed +2 spear that returns when thrown but has a dangerous haft. |
| [[Wand of Grasping Roots]]           | A wand that can cast *entangle*. |
| [[Wind-Riders Hawk Whistle]]         | A whistle that grants the user flight for as long as they can blow it. |
