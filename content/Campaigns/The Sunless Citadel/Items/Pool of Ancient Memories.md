*Wondrous item, rare*

---

This "mirror" is a handheld bowl carved from dark, petrified wood, filled with water that never seems to spill.

The holder of this bowl can learn something about the history of a specific object or creature by taking an action to gaze into the still water and think of the target. Instead of the holder's reflection, the pool presents scenes from the target's past. Information conveyed is accurate, but it is random and cryptic, and presented in no particular order, like memories bubbling to the surface.

Once it is activated, the pool gives its information for 1 minute or less, then the water clouds and returns to normal. It can't be used again until the next dawn.
