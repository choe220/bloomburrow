*Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement)*

---

This heavy bracelet is carved from a single piece of the stone-like wood of a petrified great-tree. The grain of the wood swirls in patterns that resemble screaming faces.

While you wear this bracelet, it grants you immunity to being petrified, and it allows you to cast *flesh to stone* (save DC 15) as an action. Once the spell has been cast three times, the bracelet can no longer cast it. Thereafter, you can cast *stone shape* as an action. After you have done this thirteen times, the bracelet loses its magic and crumbles to mundane dust.

***Curse.*** The bracelet's affinity with petrified wood manifests as a strange curse. Creatures of wood and plant-life (such as Barkform, Blights, and other plant creatures) have advantage on the saving throw against *flesh to stone* cast from the bracelet. If such a creature's save is successful, the bracelet breaks your attunement to it and casts the spell on you. You make your saving throw with disadvantage, and on a failed save you are instantly petrified.
