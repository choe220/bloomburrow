# Wand of Grasping Roots

*Wand, uncommon (requires attunement by a spellcaster)*

---

This wand is a gnarled length of root that feels cool to the touch. It has 7 charges.

While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 of its charges to cast the *entangle* spell (save DC 13) from it.

The wand regains 1d6 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. If you expend the wand's last charge, roll a d20. On a 1, the wand withers and crumbles into dust.
