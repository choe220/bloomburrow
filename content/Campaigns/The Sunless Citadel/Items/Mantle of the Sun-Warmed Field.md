*Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement)*

---

This elegant mantle is woven from fine, shimmering cloth in hues of red, orange, and gold, like a sunset over a summer field. It is surprisingly warm to the touch.

While you wear the mantle, you have resistance to cold damage.

In addition, you are comfortable as if the temperature were that of a balmy summer day, and you suffer no ill effects from extreme heat or cold.
