# Murky River-Stone

*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)*

---

This polished, grey-green river stone appears to be a *stone of good luck* to anyone who tries to identify it, and it confers that item's property (+1 to ability checks and saving throws) while on your person.

***Curse.*** This item is cursed. While it is on your person, you actually take a -2 penalty to ability checks and saving throws. Until the curse is discovered, the DM secretly applies this penalty, assuming you are adding a +1 bonus. You are unwilling to part with the stone until the curse is broken with *remove curse* or similar magic.
