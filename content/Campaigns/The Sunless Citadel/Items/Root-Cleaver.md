*Weapon (Longsword), uncommon (requires attunement)*

---

This longsword has a blade of darkened, hardened wood that is unnaturally sharp. A single, green vine is etched into the blade.

You have a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

If it hits an object made of wood or earth (including petrified wood, blighted plants, and creatures with bark-like skin), the hit is automatically a critical hit. Damage from nonmagical sources can't harm the weapon.
