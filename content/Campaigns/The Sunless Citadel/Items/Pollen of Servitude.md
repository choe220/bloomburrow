*Potion, uncommon*

---

This "potion" is actually a fine, shimmering powder kept in a small, airtight pouch made from a flower petal. There are several variations of this pollen, each keyed to a different creature type.

When you blow the pollen into the face of a creature within 5 feet, that creature must succeed on a DC 13 Wisdom saving throw or be affected as if by a *dominate person* spell. The effect lasts for 1 hour. The creature must be of the type targeted by the pollen.

*   **Pollen of Beast Servitude:** Affects creatures of the Beast type.
*   **Pollen of Folk Servitude:** Affects humanoids (any animalfolk).
*   **Pollen of Monster Servitude:** Affects creatures of the Monstrosity type.
