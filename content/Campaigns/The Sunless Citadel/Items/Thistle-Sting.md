*Weapon (Rapier), legendary (requires attunement)*

---

*Thistle-Sting* is a unique rapier most recently wielded by a heroic mousefolk knight. The blade is as thin as a needle and crafted from enchanted thistle-down, making it almost weightless. The handguard is a basket of woven silver thorns.

You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you hit a creature of the Monstrosity type or a creature larger than you by two or more size categories, it takes an extra 2d6 piercing damage, and it must succeed on a DC 15 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.

The sword also functions as a *wand of enemy detection*, tuned to its favored foes (Monstrosities and Large or larger creatures). It regains all expended charges at dawn.

***Sentience.*** *Thistle-Sting* is a sentient weapon of neutral good alignment, with an Intelligence of 12, a Wisdom of 12, and a Charisma of 14. It has hearing and darkvision out to a range of 120 feet. The weapon can speak and understand Sylvan and Common, and it can communicate telepathically with its wielder.

***Personality.*** This sword believes in protecting the small and allowing others to live in peace. It is protective of its friends and wants to be friends with a like-minded wielder. (It takes only 1 minute for a good-aligned character to gain attunement with the sword.) *Thistle-Sting* is courageous to the point of foolhardiness and vocally urges bold action against creatures it deems "overgrown bullies." It is likely to come into conflict with an evil or a timid wielder.
