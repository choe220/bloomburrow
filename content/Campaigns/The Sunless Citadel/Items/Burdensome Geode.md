*Wondrous item, rare*

---

This stone appears to be a beautiful geode worth 150 gp, its interior filled with glittering, sickly-purple crystals.

***Curse.*** The stone is cursed, but its magical nature is hidden; *detect magic* only reveals a faint aura of transmutation. An *identify* spell reveals the stone's true, burdensome nature.

If you use the Dash or Disengage action while the stone is on your person, its curse activates. A sense of unnatural lethargy fills you. Until the curse is broken with *remove curse* or similar magic, your speed is reduced by 5 feet, and your maximum load and maximum lift capacities are halved. You also become unwilling to part with the beautiful but heavy stone.
