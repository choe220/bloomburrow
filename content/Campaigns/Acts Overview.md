# **Act 1: Whisper of the Graveyard**

**Major Conflict:** The party confronts a rogue necromancer attempting to awaken the Bonebound, skeletal guardians of ancient calamity beasts.

**Initial Confrontation:** Upon facing the necromancer, he sneers and proclaims:
> "Fools. You trespass upon a sacred resting place, and for what? Curiosity? Foolish heroism? The Calamity Beasts slumber, but their bones still hum with power. Power wasted in stillness. But I will not let their strength go to waste. Rise, my warriors—tear the marrow from their bones. You should have turned back while you had the chance."

**Resolution:** After defeating the necromancer, they uncover fragments of writings hinting at a greater force behind the plot.

**Final Words:** As the necromancer collapses, bloodied and defiant, he whispers with a twisted grin:
>_"You… You think you've won? Fools. The dead are never truly silent. The marrow whispers, the wind carries their song. You cannot stop what has already begun… The veil thins, and beyond it, something stirs. They are listening. Watching. Waiting. He knows your names now..._”

His body twitches as if something unseen momentarily pulls at him before going limp. A cold wind rushes through the graveyard, and for just a second, the party hears faint whispers—words in a forgotten tongue—before they fade into eerie silence.

**Writings Found in the Camp:** The party uncovers scattered pages of crude parchment with unsettling text, including:
- **A letter never sent:**
	>_Elder Thornroot,  
		I have seen the truth written in the marrow of the old bones. The Calamity Beasts may have fallen, but the power they left behind is not dead. We have been blind, burying their remnants in false reverence instead of wielding them. But I have heard the whispers, and they have shown me the path forward. If you have any wisdom left, you will listen before it is too late."_
	Signed only with the initials **S.V.**

- **Torn Journal Pages**:
	- _"The Corpseberries do not grow as they once did. The roots recoil from something unseen. Even the scavengers refuse to enter the deepest tombs. Something is shifting. It is time."_
	- _"The old runes speak of a ‘Veil Beyond’ - a place where the restless dead do not dream, but wait. Could it be more than myth?"_
	- _"The Bonebound are only the first step. There must be more. There will be more."_

- **Ritual Diagram with a Scribbled Note:**
	- An ink-stained scrap of parchment contains a ritual circle, marked with unfamiliar symbols. A desperate scrawl beneath it reads:  
		_"The Eye opens. The Sleeper stirs. The Path must be prepared."_

- **Examinations of the Ritual Circle**:
	Among the scattered stones, there is a torn piece of parchment with a crude sketch of a circular ruin with standing stones. Arcane symbols around the edge resemble the sigils on the circle here
	The image is smeared with soot and blood
	"Where the stars forgot to shine, the path is marked by bone and root. The Sleeper waits beyond the gate."

# **Act 2: Unearthing the Forgotten Past**

- **Council Summons:** The party is summoned to Three-Tree City where the ruling council debates the growing reports of dark omens.
    
- **Threads to Follow:**
    
    - Investigate the necromancer’s origins, leading to old ruins and cryptic records of an ancient planar breach.
        
    - Track down the caravan attackers, revealing they belong to a growing cult known as the **Hollow Veil**, who believe the Calamity Beasts are merely dormant.
        
    - Protect settlements from emerging Bonebound threats, hinting at fractures in the boundary between planes.
        
- **Allies and Rivals:** Gain or lose favor with various council members and powerful factions, shaping the party’s resources and support.
    
- **The Revelation:** The party discovers that the Hollow Veil is attempting to reopen the breach, believing they can harness the Calamity Beasts’ power to defend Valley from an even greater threat.

# **Act 3: The Shattered Veil**

- **The Incursion:** A planar rift opens at the edge of Valley, unleashing aberrant creatures and destabilizing the land. The true villain emerges—an ancient, eldritch being that was once sealed away.
    
- **Rallying Forces:** The party must unite the council and the people of Valley to stand against the planar incursion.
    
- **The Final Confrontation:** Journeying to the rift, the party faces both the Hollow Veil cultists and the invading force. They may have to make a dire choice—to reseal the breach, destroy the ancient being, or perhaps risk attempting to control it.
    
- **Resolution:** The aftermath reshapes the politics and culture of Valley, leaving the party as legends (or infamous figures) in the land’s history.
