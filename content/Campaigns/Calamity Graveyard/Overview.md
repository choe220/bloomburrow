# Calamity Graveyard Adventure

## Overview
This adventure begins with the party receiving a quest from the council at Three-Tree City. Their mission is to investigate unusual stirrings within the Calamity Graveyard.

## Events
Upon reaching the Calamity Graveyard, the party encountered and defeated Thorne Hollow, a necromancer attempting to awaken the Bonebound. Following the confrontation, they discovered various writings hinting at a larger, unfolding plot, and a mysterious entity known as "S.V."

## Next Steps
The events in the Calamity Graveyard set the stage for Act 2: Unearthing the Forgotten Past.
