
Cautious, gentle, and unassuming, Hedgefolk are the humble gardeners and herbalists of [[Valley]]. They are small, stout folk, covered in a formidable coat of quills that belies their gentle nature. A Hedgefolk's life is one of quiet appreciation for the simple things: a well-tended garden, a warm hearth, the company of friends, and a full pantry for the [[Season of Winter|winter]]. They are slow to anger, but when their homes or loved ones are threatened, they present a surprisingly prickly and resolute defense.

Hedgefolk society is centered around peaceful, tight-knit communities, often found in quiet woodlands or rolling meadows like those near [[Goodhill]]. Their homes are cozy, well-kept burrows tucked under the roots of ancient trees or beneath sprawling hedgerows, often surrounded by meticulously-tended gardens of flowers, herbs, and vegetables.

They are masters of foraging and herbalism, possessing a deep knowledge of the plants of the [[Valley]], both mundane and magical. This makes them respected, if somewhat reclusive, members of the wider animalfolk community. A Hedgefolk's friendship is hard-won, requiring a great deal of patience, but once given, it is as steadfast and dependable as the turning of the seasons. They are not adventurers by nature, but their bravery shines brightest when they have something precious to protect.

# Location in Valley
Hedgefolk prefer quiet, pastoral lands where they can tend their gardens in peace. The largest community of Hedgefolk is in [[Goodhill]], where their cozy burrows and beautiful gardens are a common sight.

# Hedgefolk Traits
**Creature Type:** Humanoid
**Size:** Hedgefolk are stout and stand about 2 to 3 feet tall. Your size is Small.
**Speed:** Your base walking speed is 25 feet.

---
As a Hedgefolk, you have the following racial traits:

***Ability Score Increase:*** Your Constitution score increases by 2, and your Wisdom score increases by 1.

***Alignment:*** With a deep respect for community, tradition, and the simple comforts of home, hedgefolk are almost always lawful good.

***Quills:*** Your back is covered in sharp quills. When a creature within 5 feet of you hits you with a melee attack, you can use your reaction to deal piercing damage to the attacker equal to your proficiency bonus.

***Defensive Ball:*** As an action, you can curl up into a spiny ball. While curled up, you gain a +2 bonus to AC, and your speed is 0. You can use a bonus action on your subsequent turns to uncurl.

***Natural Forager:*** You have proficiency in the Nature skill.

***Languages:*** You can speak, read, and write Common.
