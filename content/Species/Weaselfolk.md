Weaselfolk are the embodiment of sleek, tenacious energy. With long, slender bodies and fur that ranges from the rich brown of riverbank soil to the pure white of winter snow, they are masters of adaptation and survival. They are a blur of motion, whether darting through the undergrowth of the [[Outer Woods]] or navigating the tangled passages of the [[Brambles]]. Weaselfolk are known for their quick wits, fierce loyalty to their kin, and an unyielding tenacity that far outweighs their small stature.

Weaselfolk society is built around tight-knit family groups or clans. These clans often inhabit sprawling, interconnected warrens dug into hillsides or the roots of large trees. They are skilled hunters and trappers, using their natural agility and cunning to catch prey and protect their territory. Their relationship with their neighbors can be complex; while fiercely protective of their own, they can be valuable allies to those who earn their trust, often serving as scouts, spies, or elite skirmishers in times of conflict.

They value cleverness and action over words, and their culture is rich with stories of cunning tricksters and relentless hunters who outsmarted much larger foes. A weaselfolk's honor is tied to their ability to provide for their family and defend their home, and they will face any danger with a startling ferocity to do so.

# Location in Valley
Weaselfolk are skilled survivors, making their homes in the wilder parts of [[Valley]]. They are most frequently encountered in the [[Outer Woods]] and the tangled passages of the [[Brambles]].

# Weaselfolk Traits
**Creature Type:** Humanoid
**Size:** Weaselfolk are slender and stand between 2 and 3 feet tall. Your size is Small.
**Speed:** Your base walking speed is 30 feet.

---
As a Weaselfolk, you have the following racial traits:

***Ability Score Increase:*** Your Dexterity score increases by 2, and your Constitution score increases by 1.

***Alignment:*** Weaselfolk are often driven by instinct and a fierce loyalty to their family or clan. They tend toward chaotic alignments, valuing their freedom and quick-wittedness.

***Sleek Build:*** You have advantage on Dexterity (Acrobatics) checks made to escape a grapple. You can also move through a space as narrow as 6 inches wide without squeezing.

***Bite:*** Your sharp teeth are a natural weapon, which you can use to make unarmed strikes. If you hit with them, you deal piercing damage equal to 1d4 + your Strength modifier, instead of the bludgeoning damage normal for an unarmed strike.

***Tenacious Hunter:*** When you damage a creature larger than you with an attack, you can use your reaction to mark them as your prey. Until the end of your next turn, you have advantage on Wisdom (Perception) and Wisdom (Survival) checks to find or track that creature. You can use this trait a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest.

***Languages:*** You can speak, read, and write Common.
