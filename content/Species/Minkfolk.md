
Bold, curious, and fiercely territorial, Minkfolk are the undisputed masters of the Valley's smaller waterways. With their sleek, dark fur that shimmers with a water-resistant sheen, they move through rivers and streams with a grace that land-dwellers can only envy. They are a blur of purposeful energy, equally at home diving for fish in the cold currents of the [[Long River]] or defending their riverbank dens from intruders. Minkfolk are resourceful and audacious, known for their sharp senses and even sharper tempers when their homes or families are threatened.

Minkfolk culture is intrinsically tied to the flow of water. They build their homes - intricate dens and lodges made of woven reeds, mud, and river stones - along the banks of rivers and the edges of marshes. While the [[Otterfolk]] may travel the main artery of the [[Long River]] in their houseboats, the Minkfolk claim the winding tributaries, creeks, and backwaters as their domain. This has led to a complex relationship between the two species, ranging from friendly rivalry and bustling trade at shared estuaries to bitter territorial disputes over prime fishing grounds.

They are exceptional anglers and trappers, using their deep knowledge of the rivers to provide for their communities. A Minkfolk's wealth and status is often measured by the quality of their fishing spots and the intricate, often fiercely guarded, system of dams and channels they build. They are wary of strangers but fiercely loyal to those who prove themselves allies, often acting as peerless guides through the Valley's most treacherous and hard-to-navigate wetlands.

# Location in Valley
Minkfolk are masters of the smaller waterways. While Otterfolk control the main artery of the [[Long River]], Minkfolk can be found in the winding tributaries, creeks, and marshes throughout [[Valley]], with a notable presence in the wetlands near [[Oakhollow Village]].

# Minkfolk Traits
**Creature Type:** Humanoid
**Size:** Minkfolk are about 3 feet tall with slender, muscular bodies. Your size is Small.
**Speed:** Your base walking speed is 30 feet, and you have a swimming speed of 30 feet.

---
As a Minkfolk, you have the following racial traits:

***Ability Score Increase:*** Your Dexterity score increases by 2, and your Wisdom score increases by 1.

***Alignment*:** Minkfolk are fiercely independent and resourceful, with a strong territorial streak. They tend toward neutral alignments, looking after their own interests and those of their clan.

***Amphibious:*** You can breathe air and water.

***Water-Repellent Fur:*** You have resistance to cold damage.

***Resourceful Forager:*** You have proficiency in the Survival skill.

***Slippery Form:*** You have advantage on ability checks and saving throws made to escape a grapple.

***Languages:*** You can speak, read, and write Common.
