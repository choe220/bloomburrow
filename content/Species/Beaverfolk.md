# Beaverfolk

![[Beaverfolk 1.png]]

Industrious, creative, and community-oriented, Beaverfolk are the master engineers and builders of the Valley. With their broad, flat tails and powerful teeth capable of felling trees, they see the natural world as a wealth of resources to be shaped and structured for the good of all. They are tireless workers who take immense pride in their creations, from sturdy dams and lodges to intricate canal systems.

Beaverfolk society is a model of collaboration and ingenuity. They live in large family groups in lodges built from mud, stones, and wood, often situated in ponds created by their own dams. These dams are engineering marvels, transforming streams and rivers to create stable, protected habitats for their communities.

They are planners and problem-solvers, always looking for ways to improve their environment. This can sometimes put them at odds with species who prefer nature to be left untamed, but the Beaverfolk genuinely believe they are improving the land for everyone's benefit. They are staunch allies in times of crisis, their well-built lodges serving as formidable fortresses against floods or attacks.

# Location in Valley
Beaverfolk are almost always found along the [[Long River]] and its many tributaries. Their ambitious dam-building projects have created a series of ponds and wetlands that are now integral to the Valley's ecosystem.

# Beaverfolk Traits
**Creature Type:** Humanoid
**Size:** Beaverfolk are stocky and strong, standing around 3 to 4 feet tall. Your size is Small.
**Speed:** Your base walking speed is 30 feet, and you have a swimming speed of 30 feet.

---
As a Beaverfolk, you have the following racial traits:

***Ability Score Increase:*** Your Constitution score increases by 2, and your Intelligence score increases by 1.

***Age:*** Beaverfolk reach adulthood around age 10 and can live up to 70 years.

***Alignment:*** Beaverfolk are naturally industrious and community-focused, with a strong sense of order. They lean heavily towards lawful alignments.

***Dam Builder:*** You are a natural engineer. You have proficiency with Carpenter's Tools. Also, whenever you make an Intelligence (Investigation) check related to the origin of structures or mechanisms, you are considered proficient in the Investigation skill and add double your proficiency bonus to the check, instead of your normal proficiency bonus.

***Lodge Dweller:*** You have advantage on saving throws against effects that would move you against your will or knock you prone while you are in contact with the ground or in water.

***Powerful Bite:*** Your large incisors are a natural weapon, which you can use to make unarmed strikes. If you hit with them, you deal piercing damage equal to 1d6 + your Strength modifier, instead of the bludgeoning damage normal for an unarmed strike.

***Languages:*** You can speak, read, and write Common.
