The Evercoat Ursine is a [[Calamity Beasts|Calamity Beast]] that appears as a large bear. At first glance, it might appear to have a brown fur coat, but its fur is actually made of leaves; a few of them are green in color, but most take the orange and red shades of autumn leaves.
