The Evercoat Ursine is a [[Calamity Beasts|Calamity Beast]] that appears as a large bear. At first glance, it might appear to have a brown fur coat, but its fur is actually made of leaves; a few of them are green in color, but most take the orange and red shades of autumn leaves.

**Season of Autumn**
Autumn is the season that the Evercoat Ursine brings. As it marches through the woods around [[Valley]], trees lose their leaves and enter a deep slumber. Other creatures also feel sluggish and sleepy in the bear's presence. It emerges infrequently, spending most of its time hibernating in large caves. The Ursine is usually woken from its sleep by the passing of [[The Sun Hawk]] that brings the summer, and once he goes to sleep, [[The Blizzard Elk]] often emerges.
