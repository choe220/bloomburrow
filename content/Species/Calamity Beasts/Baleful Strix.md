The Baleful Strix comes from somewhere beyond [[Valley]]. It is not an [[animalfolk]], but neither is it a [[Calamity Beasts|Calamity Beast]]. Some believe it is an interloper from another plane. It has the appearance of a great owl, but with wooden thorns protuding from its head and back, and wooden sticks with twine emerging from beneath its wings.

The strix was first sighted by [[squirrelfolk]] in the [[Calamity Graveyard]]. Whether or not the strix is the only of its kind remains to be seen.
