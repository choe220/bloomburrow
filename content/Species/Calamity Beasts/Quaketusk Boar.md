![[Quaketusk Boar.png]]The Quaketusk Boar is a minor [[Calamity Beasts|Calamity Beast]]. It appears as a boar with skin like molten rock, cracked with red light from beneath. Its eyes and tusks glow red with kinetic energy. Wherever the boar runs, the earth tremors like an earthquake. In the outskirts of [[Valley]], the boar's arrival has even been known to cause landslides that can bury entire villages.
