The Sun Hawk, also called the Pyreswipe Hawk, is a [[Calamity Beasts|Calamity Beast]] taking the shape of a flaming bird of prey. Travelers from other planes would liken the hawk's visage to that of a phoenix; and like the phoenix, the sun hawk bears powerful elemental fire and heat.
