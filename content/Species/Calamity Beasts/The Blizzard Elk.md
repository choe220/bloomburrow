The Blizzard Elk, also called the Galewind Moose, is a minor [[Calamity Beasts|Calamity Beast]] taking the shape of a large elk or moose. Its entire body is a shade of pale green, coated with frost, and a swirling storm of snow and ice remains suspended between its antlers.

**Season of Winter**
The Blizzard Elk is the herald of Winter. When it arrives in [[Valley]], the weather grows cold, snow begins to fall, and many [[animalfolk]] take to their burrows to rest and keep warm. When left unchecked, the Elk can bring winters cold enough to freeze the [[animalfolk]], and must be driven off by those with the courage to brave the icy frost.
