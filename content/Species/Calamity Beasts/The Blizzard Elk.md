The Blizzard Elk, also called the Galewind Moose, is a minor [[Calamity Beasts|Calamity Beast]] taking the shape of a large elk or moose. Its entire body is a shade of pale green, coated with frost, and a swirling storm of snow and ice remains suspended between its antlers.
