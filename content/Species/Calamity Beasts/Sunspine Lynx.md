The Drought Cat is a [[Calamity Beasts|Calamity Beast]] that appears a flaming lynx. A glowing sphere, like a radiant sun, rests on its back. As the cat moves, embers shoot off of it like laser beams of light.

**Season of Drought**
When the cat arrives, it brings with it a Season of Drought. It scorches the earth and ground, preventing crops from growing, and even the night is bright with radiance. Because of the nature of its shooting embers, its arrival is sometimes called the Calamity of Cinders.
