The Fecund Greenshell is a minor [[Calamity Beasts|Calamity Beast]] taking the shape of a large turtle or tortoise. It has stony skin, and its shell is covered in plants with green leaves and glowing red seeds. The greenshell is not powerful enough to bring its own season, but when it arrives, life blossoms around it. As a defense mechanism when other creatures get too close, the greenshell with fire the seeds off its shell.
