The Sandcloud Harbinger is a minor [[Calamity Beasts|Calamity Beast]] that appears as a large horse, with a brown coat and a white star on its forehead. Its eyes blaze with fire, and it is constantly surrounded by a sand storm. The elemental winds and sand are so strong, that if left unchecked, the land it moves through will be transformed into a desert.
