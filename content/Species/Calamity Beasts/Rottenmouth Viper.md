![[Rottenmouth Viper.png]]The Rottenmouth Viper is a minor [[Calamity Beasts|Calamity Beast]] taking the shape of a large snake with long fangs and black scales. Its elemental magic is not strong enough to truly bring about a season, but it remains a dangerous enemy to the [[animalfolk]]. The giant snake causes nature to decay around it. Fighting it is said to be like fighting starvation itself.
