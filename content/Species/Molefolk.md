Quiet, industrious, and deeply connected to the earth, Molefolk are the miners, excavators, and lore-keepers of the world beneath the surface. With powerful, clawed hands, and eyes unaccustomed to the bright sun, they are masters of the subterranean world. They are a sturdy and steadfast people, valuing hard work, tradition, and the wealth of secrets hidden in the soil and stone.

Molefolk society is structured around extensive, family-dug tunnel systems and underground halls. They are a communal people, working together to expand their homes, excavate valuable minerals, and protect their communities from the dangers of the deep. Their culture is one of patience and perseverance, and they have a deep respect for history and the stories that the earth itself tells.

Many Molefolk are skilled artisans, crafting beautiful jewelry from gems they unearth, or forging tools and weapons from strange subterranean metals. They are wary of the surface world, which they find overwhelmingly bright and chaotic, but they are not unfriendly. They often trade their excavated goods with surface dwellers, especially with the [[Ratfolk]], who share their appreciation for history and hidden places.

# Location in Valley
Molefolk communities are almost exclusively underground. Their largest known settlement is a network of tunnels beneath the fields of [[Goodhill]], but they can be found in any area with soft, diggable earth. They are also known to explore the [[Root Maze]] for rare minerals and historical artifacts.

# Molefolk Traits

As a Molefolk, you have the following racial traits:

**Ability Score Increase:** Your Strength score increases by 2, and your Constitution score increases by 1.

**Age:** Molefolk live for about 50 years, reaching maturity around age 10.

**Alignment:** Molefolk are hardworking and dedicated to their communities, tending to be Lawful Neutral.

**Size:** Molefolk are short and stocky, standing about 3 feet tall. Your size is Small.

**Speed:** Your base walking speed is 25 feet, and you have a burrowing speed of 15 feet.

**Darkvision:** Accustomed to life underground, you have superior vision in dark and dim conditions. You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.

**Sunlight Sensitivity:** You have disadvantage on attack rolls and on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight when you, the target of your attack, or whatever you are trying to perceive is in direct sunlight.

**Tremorsense:** You have tremorsense with a radius of 10 feet. You can detect and pinpoint the origin of vibrations within this radius, provided that you and the source of the vibrations are in contact with the same ground or substance.

**Sturdy Build:** You have advantage on saving throws against being moved against your will.

**Languages:** You can speak, read, and write Common and Terran.
