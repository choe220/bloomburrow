
Foxfolk are the embodiment of wit, charm, and quick thinking. With their sleek forms, keen eyes, and bushy tails, they move through the [[Valley]] with an undeniable grace and an air of perpetual curiosity. They are known for their intelligence, charisma, and adaptability, often finding solutions to problems that others overlook. Whether through diplomatic negotiation or clever trickery, a Foxfolk always seems to find a way.

Foxfolk society often revolves around tightly-knit family groups, though they are also known for forming larger, more loosely organized communities that value individual freedom and expression. Their homes, often hidden dens, hollowed-out logs, or cleverly disguised burrows, are found in the heart of lush forests, rolling hills, and even the edges of bustling settlements.

They possess a rich oral tradition, filled with fables, riddles, and trickster tales that highlight their reverence for wit and resourcefulness. Many Foxfolk are natural storytellers, bards, and merchants, their silver tongues making them excellent at trade and diplomacy. Others embrace a more roguish path, using their cunning for less scrupulous ventures. Regardless of their chosen profession, Foxfolk thrive on challenge and intellectual stimulation, always seeking new experiences and knowledge.

# Location in Valley
Foxfolk are adaptable and can be found in nearly any part of [[Valley]]. They often make their homes in the woods and hills bordering settlements, with significant populations thriving in the forests around [[Oakhollow Village]] and the meadows near [[Goodhill]].

# Foxfolk Traits
**Creature Type:** Humanoid
**Size:** Foxfolk are slender and agile, typically standing between 3 and 4 feet tall. Your size is Small.
**Speed:** Your base walking speed is 30 feet.

---
As a Foxfolk, you have the following racial traits:

***Ability Score Increase:*** Your Charisma score increases by 2, and your Dexterity score increases by 1.

***Alignment:*** Foxfolk often value their freedom and individuality, tending towards chaotic alignments. They can be good-hearted and friendly, but some embrace a more mischievous or self-serving nature.

***Darkvision*:** You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.

***Keen Senses:*** You have proficiency in the Perception skill.

***Silver Tongue:*** You have proficiency in either the Deception or Persuasion skill (player's choice).

***Nimble Escape:*** You can take the Disengage or Hide action as a bonus action on each of your turns.

***Languages:**** You can speak, read, and write Common.
