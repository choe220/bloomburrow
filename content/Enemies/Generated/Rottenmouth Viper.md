# Rottenmouth Viper

*Huge elemental, Neutral*

---

**Armor Class** 15
**Hit Points** 187 (22d12 + 44)
**Speed** 30 ft.

---

|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
|19 (+4)|16 (+3)|14 (+2)|1 (-5)|10 (+0)|4 (-3)|

---

**Skills** Perception +3, Stealth +6
**Damage Immunities** necrotic, poison
**Condition Immunities** exhaustion, poisoned
**Senses** blindsight 15 ft., darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 13
**Languages** ---
**Challenge** 8

---

***Blight Aura.*** Any creature that starts its turn in a 10-foot emanation originating from the viper must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a creature is exhausted.

### Actions

***Multiattack.*** The viper makes one Bite attack and uses Constrict.

***Bite.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. *Hit:* 17 (3d8 + 4) piercing damage, plus 14 (4d6) poison damage.

***Constrict.*** One Large or smaller creature the viper can see within 30 feet must make a DC 15 Strength saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 17 (3d8 + 4) bludgeoning damage and is grappled (escape DC 15).

---

> The Rottenmouth Viper is a minor Calamity Beast taking the shape of a large snake with long fangs and black scales. Its elemental magic is not strong enough to truly bring about a season, but it remains a dangerous enemy to the animalfolk. The giant snake causes nature to decay around it. Fighting it is said to be like fighting starvation itself.
