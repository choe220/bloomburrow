# Clam

*Tiny beast, Unaligned*

---

**Armor Class** 12
**Hit Points** 2 (1d4)
**Speed** 5 ft., swim 30 ft.

---

|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
|7 (-2)|10 (+0)|10 (+0)|5 (-3)|13 (+1)|8 (-1)|

---

**Condition Immunities** prone
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., tremorsense 60 ft., passive Perception 11
**Languages** ---
**Challenge** 0
**Familiar.** This creature can serve as a familiar.

---

***Water Breathing.*** The clam can breathe only underwater.

***Underwater Camouflage.*** The clam has advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made while underwater.

### Actions

***Bite.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 1 bludgeoning damage plus 1 acid damage.

***Pseudopod.*** One creature within 5 feet of the clam must succeed on a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw or take 1 bludgeoning damage. If the target is Small or smaller, it is also grappled (escape DC 12).
