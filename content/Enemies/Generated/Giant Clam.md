# Giant Clam

*Medium beast, Unaligned*

---

**Armor Class** 18
**Hit Points** 58 (9d8 + 18)
**Speed** 5 ft., swim 30 ft.

---

|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
|17 (+3)|12 (+1)|15 (+2)|5 (-3)|13 (+1)|8 (-1)|

---

**Condition Immunities** prone
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., tremorsense 60 ft., passive Perception 11
**Languages** ---
**Challenge** 2

---

***Treasure.*** A pearl worth 100 GP rests inside the giant clam.

***Water Breathing.*** The giant clam can breathe only underwater.

***Underwater Camouflage.*** The giant clam has advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made while underwater.

### Actions

***Bite.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 7 (1d8 + 3) bludgeoning damage plus 4 (1d8) acid damage.

***Pseudopod.*** One creature within 5 feet of the clam must succeed on a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or take 7 (1d8 + 3) bludgeoning damage and be grappled (escape DC 13).
