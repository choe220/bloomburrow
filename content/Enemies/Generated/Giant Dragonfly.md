# Giant Dragonfly

*Large beast, Unaligned*

---

**Armor Class** 16
**Hit Points** 22 (4d10)
**Speed** 10 ft., fly 60 ft.

---

|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
|15 (+2)|18 (+4)|8 (-1)|3 (-4)|10 (+0)|3 (-4)|

---

**Senses** passive Perception 10
**Languages** ---
**Challenge** 1/2

---

***Drone.*** When the dragonfly uses its Fly speed, it emits a loud droning sound that can be heard out to a range of 120 feet.

### Actions

***Bite.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 1 piercing damage.

### Reactions

***Uncanny Dodge.*** When an attacker that the dragonfly can see hits it with an attack, it can use its reaction to halve the attack's damage against it.
