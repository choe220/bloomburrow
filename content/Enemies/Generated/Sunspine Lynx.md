# Sunspine Lynx

*Large elemental, Unaligned*

---

**Armor Class** 17
**Hit Points** 200 (16d12 + 96)
**Speed** 50 ft., climb 40 ft.

---

|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
|15 (+2)|24 (+7)|22 (+6)|3 (-4)|14 (+2)|7 (-2)|

---

**Skills** Perception +7
**Condition Immunities** charmed, frightened, grappled, paralyzed, petrified, prone, restrained, stunned
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 17
**Languages** ---
**Challenge** 14

---

***Sun Aura.*** At the end of each of the lynx’s turns, each creature in a 10-foot emanation originating from the lynx takes 4 (1d8) radiant damage. Creatures have the blinded condition while in the emanation. Nonmagical plants in the emanation wither and die.

***Illumination.*** The lynx sheds bright light in a 60-foot radius and dim light for an additional 60 feet.

***Legendary Resistance (2/Day).*** If the lynx fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.

### Actions

***Multiattack.*** The lynx makes two Pounce attacks.

***Pounce.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +12 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. *Hit:* 14 (2d6 + 7) radiant damage.

***Hail of Cinders (Recharge 5-6).*** Each creature within 60 feet of the lynx must make a DC 19 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 55 (10d10) radiant damage and is blinded until the end of its next turn. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage.

> The Drought Cat is a Calamity Beast that appears a flaming lynx. A glowing sphere, like a radiant sun, rests on its back. As the cat moves, embers shoot off of it like laser beams of light.

> **Season of Drought.** When the cat arrives, it brings with it a Season of Drought. It scorches the earth and ground, preventing crops from growing, and even the night is bright with radiance. Because of the nature of its shooting embers, its arrival is sometimes called the Calamity of Cinders.
