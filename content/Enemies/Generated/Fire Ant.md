# Fire Ant

*Tiny beast, Unaligned*

---

**Armor Class** 11
**Hit Points** 1 (1d4 - 1)
**Speed** 10 ft.

---

|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
|1 (-5)|13 (+1)|8 (-1)|1 (-5)|7 (-2)|1 (-5)|

---

**Skills** Perception +2
**Senses** passive Perception 12
**Languages** ---
**Challenge** 1/8
**Familiar.** This creature can serve as a familiar.

---

***Heated Body.*** A creature that touches the ant or hits it with a melee attack while within 5 feet of it takes 2 (1d4) fire damage.

***Spider Climb.*** The fire ant can climb difficult surfaces, including upside down on ceilings, without needing to make an ability check.

### Actions

***Bite.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +0 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 1 piercing damage plus 1 fire damage.

***Flame Spit (Recharge 5-6).*** The ant spits fire at a creature it can see within 20 feet. The target must make a DC 11 Dexterity saving throw, taking 2 (1d4) fire damage on a failed save.
