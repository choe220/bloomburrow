# Bonebound Soldier

*Small undead, Neutral Evil*

---

**Armor Class** 16 (Bone-Plated Armor)
**Hit Points** 45 (6d8 + 18)
**Speed** 30 ft.

---

|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
|16 (+3)|12 (+1)|14 (+2)|6 (-2)|10 (+0)|5 (-3)|

---

**Saving Throws** Str +5, Con +4
**Damage Resistances** cold, necrotic
**Condition Immunities** charmed, frightened, poisoned
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 12
**Languages** Understands Common but can't speak
**Challenge** 0

---

***Revenant’s Resilience.*** If the Soldier takes more than 10 damage from a single hit, it gains a +2 bonus to its AC (up to AC 20) until the end of its next turn.

***Gravebound Strike.*** If the Soldier is within 10 feet of a tar pit or a Bonebound ally, its weapon attacks deal an additional 1d6 necrotic damage.

### Actions

***Multiattack.*** The Soldier makes two Bone Saber attacks.

***Bone Saber.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 14 (2d6 + 4) slashing damage.

---

> Risen from the tar pits of the Calamity Graveyard, these skeletal soldiers move with unnatural purpose. Their bones, blackened with ancient magic, creak as they grip rusted weapons, eyes burning with eerie, necrotic light.
