# Sun Hawk

*Large elemental, Unaligned*

---

**Armor Class** 16
**Hit Points** 142 (19d8 + 57)
**Speed** 10 ft., fly 80 ft.

---

|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
|15 (+2)|21 (+5)|17 (+3)|5 (-3)|14 (+2)|7 (-2)|

---

**Skills** Perception +10
**Damage Resistances** bludgeoning, piercing, slashing
**Damage Immunities** fire, poison
**Condition Immunities** poisoned
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 20
**Languages** ---
**Challenge** 12

---

***Fiery Aura.*** At the end of each of the hawk’s turns, each creature in a 10-foot emanation originating from the hawk takes 5 (1d10) fire damage. Creatures and flammable objects in the emanation start burning.

***Flyby.*** The sun hawk doesn't provoke opportunity attacks when it flies out of an enemy's reach.

***Illumination (Azer).*** The sun hawk sheds bright light in a 30-foot radius and dim light for an additional 30 feet.

### Actions

***Multiattack.*** The hawk makes two Talons attacks.

***Talons.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 14 (2d8 + 5) slashing damage, plus 5 (1d10) fire damage. If the target is a creature or a flammable object, it starts burning.

---

> The Sun Hawk, also called the Pyreswipe Hawk, is a Calamity Beast taking the shape of a flaming bird of prey. Travelers from other planes would liken the hawk's visage to that of a phoenix; and like the phoenix, the sun hawk bears powerful elemental fire and heat.
>
> **Season of Summer.** The Sun Hawk heralds the Season of Summer, bringing with it warmth and long days. Summer can be a good season of rest, but the hawk does not deal the summer heat in moderation; when it flies, fires start and spread, and the heroes of Valley must valiantly defend their homes if it flies too near.
>
> Treasure: None
