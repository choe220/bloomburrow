*Large beast, Unaligned*

---

**Hit Points** 22 (4d10)
**Speed** 5 ft., climb 5 ft.

|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|

**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 10
**Languages** ---
**Challenge** 1/4

---

***Salt Osmosis.*** The snail takes 5 (1d10) necrotic damage for every 5 feet it moves in salt or for every pound of salt sprinkled on it.

### Actions

***Rasp.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 3 (1d6) bludgeoning damage, plus 2 (1d4) poison damage.

***Shell Defense.*** The snail withdraws into its shell. Until it emerges, it gains a +4 bonus to AC and has advantage on Strength and Constitution saving throws. While in its shell, the snail is prone, its speed is 0 and can't increase, it has disadvantage on Dexterity saving throws, it can't take reactions, and the only action it can take is a bonus action to emerge from its shell.
