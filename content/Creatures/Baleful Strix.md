# Baleful Strix

*Large construct, Unaligned*

---

**Armor Class** 14
**Hit Points** 58 (9d10 + 9)
**Speed** 60 ft.

---

|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
|16 (+3)|18 (+4)|12 (+1)|8 (-1)|13 (+1)|10 (+0)|

---

**Skills** Perception +5, Stealth +6
**Damage Resistances** psychic
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 15
**Languages** ---
**Challenge** 4

---

***Flyby.*** The baleful strix doesn't provoke opportunity attacks when it flies out of an enemy's reach.

### Actions

***Multiattack.*** The strix makes two Talons attacks.

***Talons.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 14 (2d6 + 7) slashing damage, and the target is grappled (escape DC 13) if it is Medium or smaller.

***Screech (Recharge 5-6).*** Each creature within 60 feet that doesn't have the deafened condition must make a DC 13 Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 14 (4d6) psychic damage and is exhausted. The strix gains temporary hit points equal to the psychic damage taken.

---

> The Baleful Strix comes from somewhere beyond Valley. It is not an animalfolk, but neither is it a Calamity Beast. Some believe it is an interloper from another plane. It has the appearance of a great owl, but with wooden thorns protuding from its head and back, and wooden sticks with twine emerging from beneath its wings.
>
> The strix was first sighted by squirrelfolk in the Calamity Graveyard. Whether or not the strix is the only of its kind remains to be seen.
