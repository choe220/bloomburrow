# Nocturne Spirit

*Medium undead, Neutral*

---

**Armor Class** 12 + the spell's level
**Hit Points** 30 + 10 for each spell level 3
**Speed** 30 ft., fly 30 ft.

---

|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
|13 (+1)|16 (+3)|14 (+2)|14 (+2)|15 (+2)|15 (+2)|

---

**Damage Vulnerabilities** fire
**Damage Immunities** necrotic
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 12
**Languages** understands the languages you know and the languages it knew in life
**Challenge** --- (Proficiency Bonus equal to your Proficiency Bonus)

---

***Leaves From.*** The spirit can enter a hostile creature's space and stop there. It can move through a space as narrow as 1 inch wide.

***Plant Camouflage.*** The spirit has advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks it makes in forest terrain.

### Actions

***Multiattack.*** The spirit makes a number of Cutting Leaves attacks equal to half this spell's level (rounded down).

***Cutting Leaves.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* Bonus equals your spell attack modifier, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* (2d6 + 3) + the spell's level magical slashing damage.
