# Planned Creatures for Bloomburrow

This document lists creatures currently under consideration for integration into the Bloomburrow campaign setting. These creatures generally have a Challenge Rating (CR) of 1 or less, and are selected for their thematic fit within the world's natural and magical environments.

## Plant-Based Creatures

These creatures represent the diverse (and sometimes dangerous) flora of Bloomburrow, often found in untamed forests, marshes, and subterranean areas.

*   **Myconids (various types)**
    *   **Thematic Fit:** Reclusive fungal societies deep underground, potentially ancient or benevolent, with unique knowledge of plant magic.
    *   **CR:** Myconid Sprout (CR 0), Myconid Adult (CR 1/2)

*   **Vegepygmies (and Thornies)**
    *   **Thematic Fit:** Savage, tribal plant-folk inhabiting dense, overgrown regions like the Brambles or deep forests. They may revere powerful flora or Calamity Beasts.
    *   **CR:** Vegepygmy (CR 1/4), Thorny (CR 1/4)

*   **Violet Fungus**
    *   **Thematic Fit:** Natural hazards in damp caves, deep forest floors, or forgotten ruins.
    *   **CR:** Violet Fungus (CR 1/4)

*   **Vine Blight**
    *   **Thematic Fit:** Ambush predators in thickets, ruins, or marshlands, often connected to blighted areas or corrupted plant life.
    *   **CR:** Vine Blight (CR 1/2)

*   **Vine Blight Tangler**
    *   **Thematic Fit:** A more specialized Vine Blight, adding tactical depth to blighted encounters with its restraining abilities.
    *   **CR:** Vine Blight Tangler (CR 1/2) - (Note: This is a variant or custom creation based on Vine Blight, assuming a similar CR).

*   **Twig Blight**
    *   **Thematic Fit:** Widespread manifestations of corrupted plant life, acting as scouts or minor threats in blighted zones. Already confirmed to exist in the setting.
    *   **CR:** Twig Blight (CR 1/8)

## Elemental Creatures (Mephits)

These represent smaller, localized manifestations of elemental power, potentially echoes of the Calamity Beasts or guardians of specific elemental-infused natural sites.

*   **Dust Mephit**
    *   **Thematic Fit:** Found in arid regions, crumbling ruins, or areas affected by wind-based elemental magic.
    *   **CR:** Dust Mephit (CR 1/2)

*   **Magmin**
    *   **Thematic Fit:** Associated with geothermal activity, hot springs, volcanic vents, or areas near the Emberheart Forge.
    *   **CR:** Magmin (CR 1/2)

*   **Ice Mephit**
    *   **Thematic Fit:** Inhabiting frosty peaks, deep caves with ice formations, or areas affected by cold-based elemental magic.
    *   **CR:** Ice Mephit (CR 1/2)

*   **Steam Mephit**
    *   **Thematic Fit:** Found near hot springs, geysers, or powerful water sources, often found in regions with active hydro-magical energies.
    *   **CR:** Steam Mephit (CR 1/4)

This list will serve as a working document for integrating these creatures into the Bloomburrow setting, developing their lore, and crafting encounters.
