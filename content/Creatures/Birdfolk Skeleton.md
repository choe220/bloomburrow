# Birdfolk Skeleton

*Small undead, Lawful Evil*

---

**Armor Class** 12
**Hit Points** 13 (2d8 + 4)
**Speed** 30 ft.

---

|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
|11 (+0)|15 (+2)|14 (+2)|7 (-2)|8 (-1)|4 (-3)|

---

**Damage Vulnerabilities** bludgeoning
**Damage Immunities** poison
**Condition Immunities** exhaustion, poisoned
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 9
**Languages** Understands Common but can't speak
**Challenge** 1/4

---

***Talons.*** The birdfolk skeleton has advantage on Strength (Athletics) checks made to climb any surface its talons could reasonably grip.

### Actions

***Shortsword.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage.

***Talons.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 4 (1d4 + 2) piercing damage.
