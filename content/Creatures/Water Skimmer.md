# Water Skimmer

*Tiny beast, Unaligned*

---

**Armor Class** 11
**Hit Points** 1 (1d4 - 1)
**Speed** 20 ft.

|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|

**Senses** passive Perception 8
**Languages** ---
**Challenge** 0
**Familiar.** This creature can serve as a familiar.

---

***Water Walker.*** The skimmer can move across the surface of water as if it were solid ground. While in contact with water, the skimmer knows the exact location of any other creature in contact with the same body of water within 100 feet.

### Actions

***Bite.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 1 piercing damage.
