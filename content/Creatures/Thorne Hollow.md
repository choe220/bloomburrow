# Thorne Hollow

*Small humanoid (squirrelfolk), Lawful Evil*

---

**Armor Class** 15 (Bone Armor)
**Hit Points** 90 (12d8 + 36)
**Speed** 30 ft., climb 30 ft.

---

|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
|12 (+1)|14 (+2)|16 (+3)|18 (+4)|14 (+2)|12 (+1)|

---

**Saving Throws** Wis +5, Con +7
**Skills** Arcana +7, Stealth +5
**Damage Resistances** cold, necrotic
**Condition Immunities** charmed, frightened, poisoned
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 13
**Languages** Common, Primordial, Squirrel
**Challenge** 4

---

***Bonebound Summoning (1/day).*** At the start of combat, Thorne raises two Bonebound Soldiers from the ground. These soldiers take their turns immediately after Thorne.

***Undead Fortitude.*** If Thorne is reduced to 0 HP, he makes a Constitution saving throw with a DC of 10 + the damage taken. On a success, he drops to 1 HP instead of dying.

### Spellcasting
Thorne is a gifted spellcaster. His spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 15). He can cast the following spells:
- **At will:** *chill touch, mage hand, minor illusion*
- **2/day each:** *darkness, misty step, ray of enfeeblement*
- **1/day each:** *animate dead, blight, vampiric touch*

### Actions

***Multiattack.*** Thorne makes two Bonebound Scythe attacks or casts a spell.

***Bonebound Scythe.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 14 (2d8 + 4) slashing damage, plus 5 (1d10) necrotic damage.

***Dark Tendrils (Recharge 5-6).*** Black necrotic energy lashes out in a 20-ft. cone. Creatures in the area must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw, taking 24 (6d8) necrotic damage on a failure, or half as much on a success.

### Reactions

***Grim Harvest (1/Turn).*** When Thorne kills a creature with necrotic damage, he can take a Reaction to regain 9 (2d8) hit points.

***Featherfall (1/Turn).*** When Thorne falls at least 10 feet above the ground, he can take a Reaction to take 0 damage from the fall.

---

> A master of forbidden necromancy, this twisted Squirrelfolk channels the restless power of the Calamity Beasts’ remains. With every whispered incantation, the ground churns, birthing new horrors from the tar-soaked bones of the past.
