# Blizzard Elk

*Large elemental, Neutral*

---

**Armor Class** 15
**Hit Points** 189 (14d12 + 98)
**Speed** 40 ft.

---

|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
|25 (+7)|14 (+2)|24 (+7)|2 (-4)|12 (+1)|6 (-2)|

---

**Damage Resistances** bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks
**Damage Immunities** cold
**Condition Immunities** charmed, frightened, grappled, paralyzed, petrified, prone, restrained, stunned
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 11
**Languages** ---
**Challenge** 12

---

***Chilling Aura.*** At the end of each of the elk's turns, each creature in a 10-foot emanation originating from the elk takes 5 (1d10) cold damage. Creatures and flammable objects in the emanation start burning. Nonmagical liquids in the emanation that aren't being worn or carried freeze.

***Magic Resistance.*** The elk has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

### Actions

***Multiattack.*** The elk makes two Ram attacks.

***Ram.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +11 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 17 (1d12 + 11) bludgeoning damage plus 6 (1d12) cold damage. If the elk moved at least 20 feet straight toward the target immediately before the hit, the target takes an extra 7 (2d6) bludgeoning damage and, if it is Huge or smaller, is knocked prone.

***Winter's Breath (Recharge 5-6).*** Each creature in a 60-foot cone must make a DC 17 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 27 (5d10) cold damage and is restrained until the start of the elk's next turn. If the save fails by 5 or more, the creature is also incapacitated while restrained. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage, and its speed is reduced by 10 feet until the start of the elk's next turn.

---

> The Blizzard Elk, also called the Galewind Moose, is a minor Calamity Beast taking the shape of a large elk or moose. Its entire body is a shade of pale green, coated with frost, and a swirling storm of snow and ice remains suspended between its antlers.
>
> **Season of Winter.** The Blizzard Elk is the herald of Winter. When it arrives in Valley, the weather grows cold, snow begins to fall, and many animalfolk take to their burrows to rest and keep warm. When left unchecked, the Elk can bring winters cold enough to freeze the animalfolk, and must be driven off by those with the courage to brave the icy frost.
