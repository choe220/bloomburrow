**Languages** ---

**Familiar.** This creature can serve as a familiar.

### Actions

***Secrete Poison.*** One creature the firefly can see within 5 feet of it must succeed on a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw or take 1 poison damage.

***Illumination.*** Until the start of its next turn, the firefly sheds bright light in a 15-foot radius and dim light for an additional 15 feet.
