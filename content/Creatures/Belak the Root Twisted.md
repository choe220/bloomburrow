### Belak the Root-Twisted
*Medium humanoid (Raccoonfolk), Neutral Evil*
**Armor Class** 16 (Barkskin)
**Hit Points** 99 (18d8 + 18)
**Speed** 30 ft.

| STR     | DEX     | CON     | INT     | WIS     | CHA     |
|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| 12 (+1) | 14 (+2) | 13 (+1) | 16 (+3) | 18 (+4) | 15 (+2) |

**Saving Throws** Int +6, Wis +7
**Skills** Nature +6, Perception +7, Survival +7
**Damage Resistances** Poison
**Condition Immunities** Charmed, Frightened
**Senses** Passive Perception 17
**Languages** Common, Druidic, Sylvan
**Challenge** 6 (2,300 XP)

***Spellcasting.*** Belak is a 9th-level spellcaster. His spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 15, +7 to hit with spell attacks). He has the following druid spells prepared:
Cantrips (at will): *Druidcraft, Poison Spray, Shillelagh*
1st level (4 slots): *Cure Wounds, Entangle, Faerie Fire, Thunderwave*
2nd level (3 slots): *Barkskin, Flaming Sphere, Pass without Trace*
3rd level (3 slots): *Call Lightning, Plant Growth*
4th level (3 slots): *Blight, Grasping Vine*
5th level (1 slot): *Wrath of Nature*

***Circle of Spores.*** Belak is surrounded by a cloud of necrotic spores. As a bonus action, he can deal 1d6 necrotic damage to one creature he hits with a weapon attack. He can also use his reaction to deal 1d6 necrotic damage to a creature that ends its turn within 10 feet of him.

#### Actions
**Staff.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 4 (1d6 + 1) bludgeoning damage, or 5 (1d8 + 1) bludgeoning damage with *Shillelagh*.
