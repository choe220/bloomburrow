# Creatures of Bloomburrow

Welcome to the bestiary for the world of Bloomburrow. This page serves as a central index for some of the known creatures that inhabit the vibrant and sometimes dangerous lands of the [[Valley]] and beyond.

The world of Bloomburrow is teeming with a diverse array of life, from the smallest insects to the colossal [[Calamity Beasts]] that shape the seasons. The creatures are as varied as the [[animalfolk]] who live here, each with their own unique place in the ecosystem.

This compendium provides information on:
*   **Beasts:** The natural wildlife of Bloomburrow, including common animals and their giant counterparts.
*   **Elementals & [[Calamity Beasts]]:** Powerful and primal forces of nature that can bring both creation and destruction.
*   **Humanoids:** Various folk, friends and foes.
*   **Undead:** Creatures that defy the natural cycle of life and death.
*   **And more...**
