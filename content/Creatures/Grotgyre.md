# Grotgyre

*Large beast, Chaotic Neutral*

---

**Armor Class** 16
**Hit Points** 52 (5d10 + 25)
**Speed** 10 ft.

---

|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
|16 (+3)|5 (-3)|20 (+5)|10 (+0)|17 (+3)|5 (-3)|

---

**Damage Immunities** psychic
**Senses** passive Perception 16
**Languages** telepathy 120 ft.
**Challenge** 3

---

***Salt Osmosis.*** Grotgyre takes 5 (1d10) necrotic damage for every 5 feet it moves in salt or for every pound of salt sprinkled on him.

### Actions

***Multiattack.*** Grotgyre makes two Rasp attacks.

***Rasp.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 10 (2d6 + 3) slashing damage.

***Shell Defense.*** Grotgyre withdraws into its shell. Until it emerges, it gains a +4 bonus to AC and has advantage on Strength and Constitution saving throws. While in its shell, Grotgyre is prone, its speed is 0 and can't increase, it has disadvantage on Dexterity saving throws, it can't take reactions, and the only action it can take is a bonus action to emerge from its shell.

### Spellcasting (Psionics)
Grotgyre casts one of the following spells, requiring no components, using Wisdom as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 15):
- **At will:** *charm person, crown of madness, detect thoughts, dissonant whispers*
- **1/day each:** *confusion, modify memory, suggestion*

### Reactions

***Alien Mind.*** *Trigger:* A creature tries to read Grotgyre's thoughts or deal Psychic damage to it. *Response:* The triggering creature must make a DC 15 Intelligence saving throw. On a failed save, the target is stunned. At the end of each of its turns, the target repeats the save, ending the effect on itself on a success. After 1 minute, it succeeds automatically.

---

> Grotgyre is a large, sapient snail with hypnotic power strong enough to bend the minds of animalfolk who get close. He has forged a psychic connection with the Ratfolk Warlock Wick, and together they command a cadre of snail-worshippers from labyrinthine tunnels below Valley. See also Wicks' statblock.
>
> **Great Old One.** Grotgyre's history and origin are unknown, but his psychic power makes him an excellent Great Old One patron for a Warlock character. His servants include Snails, along with Cultists and Cult Fanatics from every animalfolk species.
