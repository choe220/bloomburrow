# Character Creation Guide

This guide will walk you through creating your own [[animalfolk]] character to explore the world of [[Valley]]. Follow these steps to build your character.

## 1. Choose Your Species

The first step is to choose what kind of [[animalfolk]] you want to be. The various species each have unique traits and cultures.

-   **[[Species/Animalfolk|List of All Animalfolk]]**: A comprehensive list of the various animal species that inhabit the world of Bloomburrow.

Some of the playable species include:
-   [[Species/Axolotlfolk|Axolotlfolk]]: Rare, exotic, and aquatic beings from beyond the [[Brambles]], with powerful regenerative abilities.
-   [[Species/Badgerfolk|Badgerfolk]]: Solitary, peaceable, and formidable wanderers, often misunderstood for their fierce loyalty and strength.
-   [[Species/Batfolk|Batfolk]]: Nocturnal guardians and astronomers who read the cosmos and protect the vulnerable with sound-based magic.
-   [[Species/Beaverfolk|Beaverfolk]]: Industrious and community-oriented engineers who shape the land by building dams and lodges.
-   [[Species/Birdfolk|Birdfolk]]: Chivalrous and idealistic leaders who connect [[Valley]]'s communities and master the art of flight.
-   [[Species/Foxfolk|Foxfolk]]: Witty, charming, and adaptable tricksters known for their intelligence and silver tongues.
-   [[Species/Frogfolk|Frogfolk]]: Curious and intelligent water mages and augurs who can glimpse the future to protect [[Valley]].
-   [[Species/Hedgehogs|Hedgehogs]]: Cautious, gentle, and unassuming gardeners and herbalists with a surprisingly prickly defense.
-   [[Species/Lizardfolk|Lizardfolk]]: Passionate and resilient artists and fire mages who worship the cycle of creation and destruction.
-   [[Species/Minkfolk|Minkfolk]]: Bold, curious, and fiercely territorial masters of [[Valley]]'s smaller waterways and wetlands.
-   [[Species/Molefolk|Molefolk]]: Quiet, industrious, and steadfast miners and lore-keepers of the subterranean world.
-   [[Species/Mousefolk|Mousefolk]]: Plucky, loyal, and sociable fighters who value family and adventure in equal measure.
-   [[Species/Otterfolk|Otterfolk]]: Playful, thrill-seeking, and adventurous river-dwellers who chase storms and live on houseboats.
-   [[Species/Possumfolk|Possumfolk]]: Jovial and nomadic explorers who wander the wilds with their families, uncovering hidden mysteries.
-   [[Species/Rabbitfolk|Rabbitfolk]]: Stalwart, hospitable, and community-focused farmers and chefs who provide for [[Valley]].
-   [[Species/Raccoonfolk|Raccoonfolk]]: Free-spirited wanderers and collectors who are proficient with improvised weapons and trash magic.
-   [[Species/Ratfolk|Ratfolk]]: Reclusive and thoughtful keepers of secrets and ancient relics who form psychic bonds with insects.
-   [[Species/Skunkfolk|Skunkfolk]]: Infamous for their powerful musk, these folk often live on the fringes, sharing a special bond with [[batfolk]].
-   [[Species/Snakefolk|Snakefolk]]: Rarely seen and often feared, these large, powerful folk possess a venomous bite and constricting grip.
-   [[Species/Squirrelfolk|Squirrelfolk]]: Morbid and practical stewards of the life-death cycle who preserve food and ritually bury the dead.
-   [[Species/Turtlefolk|Turtlefolk]]: Incredibly long-lived, patient, and methodical solitaires who possess ancient wisdom and a tough natural defense.
-   [[Species/Weaselfolk|Weaselfolk]]: Sleek, tenacious, and fiercely loyal hunters who are masters of adaptation and survival in the wilder parts of [[Valley]].

## 2. Choose Your Home Settlement

Your character's home shapes their perspective on the world. The six major settlements on the Valley Council offer diverse environments:

-   [[Places/Fountainport/Fountainport|Fountainport]]: A three-tiered city carved from soapstone, rising from a large pond and home to most of the [[Valley]]'s [[frogfolk]].
-   [[Places/Oakhollow/Oakhollow Village|Oakhollow Village]]: A village built within the trunk and branches of an ancient oak tree, bordering the mysterious [[Calamity Graveyard]].
-   [[Places/Cactus Point Village/Cactus Point Village|Cactus Point Village]]: A resilient oasis village built among massive cacti in a harsh desert, fiercely guarding its hidden wellspring.
-   [[Places/Goodhill/Goodhill|Goodhill]]: A diverse and picturesque town of clay burrows and white-petal roofs, known for its large windmill and cobbled streets.
-   [[Places/Rockface Village/Rockface Village|Rockface Village]]: A rugged settlement of [[lizardfolk]] carved into a cliffside, home to the legendary [[Emberheart Forge]].
-   [[Places/Lupinflower Village|Lupinflower Village]]: A small, peaceful village of [[rabbitfolk]] and [[mousefolk]], known for its fields of vibrant lupin flowers.

## 3. Choose Your Class & Subclass

Your class determines your character's abilities. Start by reviewing the available classes, and then choose a more specialized subclass.

-   **[[Culture/Classes]]**: A summary of how standard RPG classes like Barbarian, Druid, and Fighter are uniquely adapted to the world of Bloomburrow.

Some available subclasses include:
-   [[Culture/Subclasses/Circle of Wildfire|Circle of Wildfire]]: For Druids who understand that destruction precedes creation, bonding with a primal spirit of fire to control flames that both burn and heal.
-   [[Culture/Subclasses/Echo Knight|Echo Knight]]: A martial path for Fighters, often [[mousefolk]], who use weaving magic to create ethereal duplicates of themselves in battle.
-   [[Culture/Subclasses/Necromancer|Necromancer]]: For Wizards who study the manipulation of life energy, seeing themselves as recyclers of power rather than villains.
-   [[Culture/Subclasses/Omenpath Walker|Omenpath Walker]]: A Ranger archetype that harnesses the wild, unpredictable magic of the Omenpaths that connect different planes.
-   [[Culture/Subclasses/Storm Sorcery|Storm Sorcery]]: A Sorcerous Origin where innate magic is fueled by the power of elemental air, often traced back to a [[Calamity Beast]].

## 4. Choose Your Background

Your background represents your life and occupation before becoming an adventurer. It grants you specific skills and features.

-   **[[Culture/Backgrounds]]**: A list of professions and life experiences from which you can choose to define your character's history.

Available backgrounds include:
-   [[Culture/Backgrounds/Acolyte|Acolyte]]: You served a church or grove, honoring ancestors and the Cosmos under the tutelage of a Cleric.
-   [[Culture/Backgrounds/Archivist|Archivist]]: You spent your youth in the [[ratfolk]]'s [[Knowledge Ossuary]], studying artifacts and forgotten lore.
-   [[Culture/Backgrounds/Artisan|Artisan]]: You learned a trade in a workshop, mastering a craft and developing a keen eye for detail and value.
-   [[Culture/Backgrounds/Caretaker|Caretaker]]: You have a natural knack for helping in dire situations, learning to apply medicine and care to all species.
-   [[Culture/Backgrounds/Chef|Chef]]: Raised in a kitchen, you have an innate intuition for combining ingredients into delicious dishes.
-   [[Culture/Backgrounds/Criminal|Criminal]]: You survived by cutting purses or burgling shops, perhaps in the alleys of a city or as a road bandit.
-   [[Culture/Backgrounds/Entertainer|Entertainer]]: You thrive on applause, having learned your performance skills from [[Batfolk]] bards or [[Otterfolk]] acrobats.
-   [[Culture/Backgrounds/Farmer|Farmer]]: You grew up close to the land, cultivating the earth and gaining a deep respect for nature's bounty and wrath.
-   [[Culture/Backgrounds/Fisherman|Fisherman]]: Your life was spent on the water, learning the art of fishing and the promise of the eternal horizon.
-   [[Culture/Backgrounds/Fortune Teller|Fortune Teller]]: Mentored by a [[Frogfolk]] auger or [[Batfolk]] stargazer, you learned to see the patterns of the future.
-   [[Culture/Backgrounds/Guard|Guard]]: You spent countless hours in a guard tower, watching for [[Calamity Beasts]] outside and troublemakers inside.
-   [[Culture/Backgrounds/Guide|Guide]]: You came of age in the wilderness, learning to fend for yourself while exploring ruins and hunting beasts.
-   [[Culture/Backgrounds/Hunter|Hunter]]: You are a skilled tracker and patient stalker, living off the land and hunting beasts in the wilds.
-   [[Culture/Backgrounds/Innkeeper|Innkeeper]]: You learned the business of hospitality, how to run an inn, and how to sweet-talk your patrons.
-   [[Culture/Backgrounds/Merchant|Merchant]]: Apprenticed to a trader, you learned the art of commerce by buying and selling goods across [[Valley]].
-   [[Culture/Backgrounds/Noble|Noble]]: Raised as an heir to a local leader, you received a first-class education in history and diplomacy.
-   [[Culture/Backgrounds/Sailor|Sailor]]: You grew up on the [[Long River]], facing mighty storms, enormous creatures, and even pirate crews.
-   [[Culture/Backgrounds/Scavenger|Scavenger]]: Growing up on the fringes, you learned to take what's been discarded, never letting anything go to waste.
-   [[Culture/Backgrounds/Soldier|Soldier]]: You joined a mercenary company or knightly order, and battle is now in your blood.
