title: Welcome to Valley
---
![[Overlooking Valley.jpg]]
The land of **[[Valley]]** is a place of **untamed beauty and quiet wonders,** where the **[[Long River]]** winds its way through **rolling meadows, shadowed thickets, and misty marshlands.** Here, **[[animalfolk]] thrive**, carving out lives in burrows, tree-homes, and bustling towns woven into the landscape itself.

The **[[animalfolk]] of [[Valley]]** are as varied as the land itself—**[[Rabbitfolk]] messengers dart across the meadows, [[Otterfolk]] ride their houseboat rafts along the river, and [[Lizardfolk]] bask on sun-warmed stones of the hills.** The clever **[[Ratfolk]] archivists** hoard knowledge in their subterranean libraries, while the **[[Squirrelfolk]] Weavers** shape the flow of magic with their ancestral loomcraft. **[[Molefolk]] tunnel unseen beneath the [[Brambles]], [[Birdfolk]] glide on the valley’s winds, and [[Frogfolk]] watch the stars reflected in their still ponds.** Each community carries its own traditions, superstitions, and ambitions, but together they form the intricate tapestry of life in [[Valley]].

Yet, beneath its pastoral charm, [[Valley]] is a land shaped by **ancient forces**. The towering **[[Calamity Beasts]]**, embodiments of the seasons, once roamed freely—**great storms given form, raging tides given hunger.** Now, their bones rest in sacred graveyards, their power lingering in the roots and rivers.

Among [[Valley]]’s greatest traditions is **[[Weaving]],** an ancient practice of working magic by stitching together the unseen threads of fate. Those who master this craft—**Weavers**—can mend wounds, alter luck, or even bend reality itself. The **[[Squirrelfolk]]** are the most renowned Weavers, their enchanted tapestries used for everything from blessing crops to binding spirits. However, some fear the forbidden branches of the craft—**dark [[Weaving]], which can twist life and death itself.** It is said that those who pull too hard on fate’s threads may unravel something best left untouched.

The cities and villages of [[Valley]] each hold their own character. The **towering willows of [[Three-Tree City]]** rise from where the river meets the sea, a hub of commerce and governance. **[[Fountainport]]’s cascading tiers** shimmer above its great pond, while **[[Goodhill]]’s woven-grass homes** sway gently in the wind. And in the deep places, where few dare tread, lie the forgotten corners of the world—like the **[[Calamity Graveyard]],** where whispers of the past still stir.

This is a land of **tradition and change**, of old magic and new ambitions. A land where **heroes** are measured not by their size, but by their **cleverness, bravery, and heart.**

And today, whether by fate or by choice, **your story is about to be woven into the fabric of [[Valley]] itself.**
