[[Beza]] brings the season of Spring. This season is characterized by a resurgence of growth and new life. It is one of the annual seasons; When Beza arrives, it drives off [[The Blizzard Elk]] of [[Season of Winter|Winter]], allowing plants to flourish. New life for plants also means renewed life for many hibernating insects, and these insects help pollinate many flowers; much to the chagrin of those with allergies. Many [[animalfolk]] plant their crops in Spring, hoping to reap them come the season of Harvest.
