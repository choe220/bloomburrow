Autumn is the season that [[The Evercoat Ursine]] brings. As it marches through the woods around [[Valley]], trees lose their leaves and enter a deep slumber. Other creatures also feel sluggish and sleepy in the bear's presence. It emerges infrequently, spending most of its time hibernating in large caves. The [[The Evercoat Ursine|Ursine]] is usually woken from its sleep by the passing of [[The Sun Hawk]] that brings the [[Season of Summer|summer]], and once he goes to sleep, [[The Blizzard Elk]] often emerges.
