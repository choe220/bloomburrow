# Martial Archetype: Echo Knight

The mousefolk of Bloomburrow are among the smallest, but also the mightiest. Some skilled mousefolk have studied a form of weaving that allows them to create ethereal duplicates of themselves, finding strength in numbers even when they face enemies alone.

---

### Level 3: Manifest Echo
As a bonus action, you can summon an ethereal echo of yourself in an unoccupied space you can see within 15 feet of yourself. The echo disappears when it is reduced to 0 Hit Points, when you use this feature to summon the echo again, when you take a bonus action to dismiss it, when you have the incapacitated condition, or when you die. The echo also disappears at the end of your turn if it is more than 30 feet away from you.

The echo is a Medium object with the following statistics:
- **Armor Class:** 14 + your proficiency bonus
- **Hit Points:** 1
- **Speed:** 30 ft., fly 30 ft. (hover)
- **Immunities:** Poison damage, psychic damage, and all conditions.

While the echo persists, you can use it in the following ways:
- On your turn, you can mentally command the echo to move up to its Speed (no action required).
- As a bonus action, you can spend 15 feet of movement to teleport, swapping spaces with your echo.
- When you take the Attack action on your turn, any attack you make with that action can originate from your space or the echo's space. You make this choice for each attack.
- When a creature that you can see within 5 feet of your echo moves at least 5 feet away from it, you can take a reaction to make an Opportunity Attack against that creature as if you were in the echo's space.

### Level 3: Unleash Incarnation
You can heighten your echo's fury. Whenever you take the Attack action, you can make one additional melee attack from the echo's position. You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Constitution modifier (a minimum of once), and regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest.

### Level 7: Echo Avatar
As a Magic action, you can see through the echo's eyes and hear through its ears as if you were in its space for up to 10 minutes, and you can end this effect at any time (no action required). While your echo is being used in this way, it can be up to 1,000 feet away from you without being destroyed.

### Level 10: Ethereal Martyr
You can make your echo throw itself in front of an attack directed at another creature that you can see within 30 feet. Before the attack roll is made, you can take a reaction to teleport the echo to an unoccupied space within 5 feet of the target. The attack roll that triggered the reaction is instead made against your echo.

Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a short or long rest.

### Level 15: Reclaim Potential
You've learned to absorb the fleeting magic of your echo. When your echo is reduced to 0 Hit Points, you can gain a number of temporary hit points equal to 2d6 + your Constitution modifier.

You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Constitution modifier (a minimum of once). You regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest.

### Level 18: Legion of One
You can take a bonus action to create two echoes with your Manifest Echo feature, and these echoes can co-exist. If you try to create a third echo, the previous two echoes disappear. Anything you can do from one echo's position can be done from the other's instead.

In addition, when you roll initiative and have no uses of your Unleash Incarnation feature left, you regain one use of that feature.
