Each [[animalfolk]] species has its own unique food culture that favors distinct flavors and ingredients. Typically, [[animalfolk]] are vegetarians, though some, like [[otterfolk]], eat ﬁsh, while insects are occasionally enjoyed as part of larger meals. Known dishes served in [[Valley]] include:

- Acorn scones
- Beetle crackers
- Blackberry punch
- Carrot Cake
- Carrot muffins
- Chamomile fizzy drink
- Chamomile tea
- Cherry jam
- Dandelion and turnip green salad
- Elderberry jam thumbprint cookies
- Mint tea
- Grilled minnows
- Mugwort tea
- Raspberry jam
- Seedcake or seedloaf
- Strawberry cake
- Strawberry cookies
- Strawberry crumble
- Strawberry rhubarb muffins
- Strawberry tarts
- Sunflower seed scones
- Treacle
