The use of magic, known as "weaving," is common among the [[animalfolk]]. It is derived from nature and is often passed culturally or through the family. More powerful magic is generally avoided, as it has been known to corrupt its weavers, turning them into monsters fueled by rage. Some believe this may be the origin of the [[Calamity Beasts]].

Each [[animalfolk]] uses unique forms of weaving. [[Otterfolk|Otters]] can influence the currents of the rivers. [[Lizardfolk|Lizards]] use fire magic, and [[Frogfolk|frogs]] water magic and augury. Among the [[squirrelfolk]], there are necromancers. [[Mousefolk]] are expert at using spells to augment their speed and strength. They use a form of weaving known as blurring, where they move so fast that they appear as stuttering afterimages, giving the impression that there are many [[mousefolk]] attacking their enemy.
