Otterball is a chaotic, ever-evolving sport played in lakes, rivers, and ocean shallows by [[otterfolk]]. The game is endless, and ongoing. The exact rules are unknown and the parameters are constantly shift. The game involves a charged orb of lightning, said to be distilled from a [[Calamity Beasts|Calamity Beast]]’s storm, which skips and arcs across the water unpredictably. Players attempt to pass, bounce, or submerge the ball while evading zaps, setting up elaborate trick shots, and executing dramatic, mid-air dives. The rules seem to shift depending on the game’s current momentum, the whims of the players, and even environmental factors like the tide or rainfall. Outsiders are often baffled, but [[otterfolk]] always seem to instinctively understand the ever-changing play.
