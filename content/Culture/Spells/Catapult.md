# Catapult

*1st-level transmutation*

**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 60 feet
**Components:** S
**Duration:** Instantaneous

---

Choose a nonmagical object weighing 1 to 5 pounds within range that isn't being worn or carried. The target flies in a straight line up to 30 feet in a direction you choose before falling to the ground, stopping early if it strikes a solid surface.

If the target would strike a creature, that creature must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or be struck. When the target strikes something, the target and what it strikes each take {@dice 1d8} Bludgeoning damage for every 10 feet the target moved.

### At Higher Levels
The distance the target flies increases by 10 feet for each spell slot level above 1.

---
**Classes:** Druid, Wizard, Ranger
