# The Bloomburrow Calendar

The passage of time in Valley is marked by the turning of the seasons and the phases of its single moon. The year is comprised of 12 months, each lasting 28 days, for a total of 336 days.

Each month corresponds to a full cycle of the moon, and is broken down into four 7-day weeks, with each week marking a lunar phase (New, Waxing, Full, Waning). The [[Lunar Paean]] ceremony is held at the end of each of these phases.

## Days of the Week

The days of the week are named for the small, important parts of life in the wood.

- **Seed-day** (First day of the week)
- **Sprout-day**
- **Bloom-day**
- **Peak-day**
- **Fade-day**
- **Still-day**
- **Root-day** (Last day of the week)

## Months of the Year

The twelve months are grouped into the four primary seasons.

### Season of Spring (Brought by Beza)
*   **1. Month of Thawing**
*   **2. Month of Firstleaf**
*   **3. Month of Rains**

### Season of Summer (Brought by The Sun Hawk)
*   **4. Month of Sunbright**
*   **5. Month of High-heat**
*   **6. Month of Berries**

### Season of Autumn (Brought by The Evercoat Ursine)
*   **7. Month of Harvest**
*   **8. Month of Goldleaf**
*   **9. Month of Last Bite**

### Season of Winter (Brought by The Blizzard Elk)
*   **10. Month of First Frost**
*   **11. Month of Deepwood**
*   **12. Month of Stillness**

## The Great Seasons

Beyond the four yearly seasons, the world of Bloomburrow experiences rare, powerful seasons that can last for years or even decades at a time, each heralded by a mighty Calamity Beast. These "Great Seasons" are not part of the yearly calendar but are defining eras for those who live through them. They are:

*   [[Season of Deep Waters]] (Heralded by Eluge)
*   [[Season of Drought]] (Heralded by The Sunspine Lynx, also known as Drought Cat)
*   [[Season of Flames]] (Heralded by Wildsear)
*   [[Season of Long Night]] (Heralded by Maha)
*   [[Season of Salt]] (Heralded by Ygra)
*   [[Season of the Maze]] (Heralded by Lumra)
