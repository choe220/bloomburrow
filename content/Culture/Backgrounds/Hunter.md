# Hunter

- **Ability Scores:** Strength, Dexterity, Wisdom
- **Feat:** {@feat Skulker}
- **Skill Proficiencies:** {@skill Stealth}, {@skill Survival}
- **Tool Proficiency:** Leatherworker's Tools
- **Equipment:** _Choose A or B:_ (A) Longbow, 20 arrows, a hunting trap, a trophy from a beast you've slain, traveler's clothes, and a pouch with 10 GP; or (B) 50 GP

---

You've spent your life in the wilds of the Brambles, living off the land and hunting the beasts that roam the forests. You are a skilled tracker and a patient stalker, and you know the ways of the wild better than any city-dweller. You may have hunted for food, for sport, or to protect nearby settlements from dangerous creatures.
