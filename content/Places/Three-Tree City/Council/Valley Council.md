The Valley Council is the loose governing body of [[Valley]]. They generally oversee communication between the individual [[Animalfolk|animalfolk]] governances.

The current council in [[Three-Tree City]] consists of [[Counselor Fleetpaw]], [[Chronicler Tibbins]], [[Guildmaster Crenz]], [[High Warden Thrynn]], [[Oracle Etrix]], [[Viscount Brindlewood]], and [[Captain Brisken]].
