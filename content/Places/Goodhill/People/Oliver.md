Oliver, the small, cinnamon-brown [[rabbitfolk]] mayor of [[Goodhill]], is a charismatic and flamboyant figure who stands out despite his diminutive stature. Adorned in a bright blue vest and yellow cravat, he combines a sense of dignity with an approachable, if somewhat theatrical, demeanor. Oliver's round, expressive eyes and reedy voice make him a memorable presence. His natural curiosity and slight nosiness drive him to be deeply involved in the town's affairs. However, his tendency to downplay serious concerns, like the threat of the [[Species/Calamity Beasts/Maha|Night Owl]], reveals a certain naivety. Despite his good-hearted intentions and dedication to his community, Oliver's love for grand gestures and embellishments sometimes makes him appear insincere or dismissive. Nevertheless, his charm and genuine admiration for his fellow townsfolk keep him well-liked and central to [[Goodhill]]'s social life.
