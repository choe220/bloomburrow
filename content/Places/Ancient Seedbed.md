The Ancient Seedbed is an untamed wilderness of wild flora, sprawling with overgrown fields and dense thickets where unique and unrefined plants flourish. Unlike the cultivated farmlands of [[Valley]], the seedbed is a chaotic patchwork of varying plant species, from wild herbs and root vegetables to strange fruit-bearing bushes and towering, twisted vines. The air is thick with the scent of earth and greenery, and the land is marked by an abundance of rare, hardy plant life that thrives without human interference. Though the food grown here isn’t as refined or predictable as that in [[Valley]], it is rich in variety and resilience, offering a bounty of unusual produce with distinctive flavors and properties.

The [[rabbitfolk]] of [[Valley]] have long made use of the seedbed, carefully harvesting the plants and crossbreeding them with other varieties to enhance their yield, flavor, and size. Through generations of careful cultivation, they have learned to turn these wild, often unpredictable plants into valuable crops that feed [[Valley]]’s people. The seedbed serves as the primary source of many of the seeds that are planted across [[Valley]], providing a diverse array of produce that can’t be found anywhere else. Despite its wildness, the Ancient Seedbed is an essential part of [[Valley]]’s agricultural cycle, fueling the creativity and adaptability of the [[rabbitfolk]] who tend to it.
