Pondside is a vibrant, communal settlement nestled along the banks of [[Pond Creek]] and [[Lilyglow Pond]], sitting between [[Haymeadow]] and [[Dewrim]] on the main road near [[Goodhill]]. The village is a collection of raised wooden walkways, stilted houses, and floating platforms designed to accommodate the amphibious nature of its [[frogfolk]] majority. The homes are built from woven reeds, driftwood, and packed mud, their roofs thatched with watergrass and lily fronds. Brightly colored banners and lanterns sway from wooden poles, creating a warm and inviting atmosphere.

The village layout is organic and fluid, with narrow boardwalks crisscrossing the marshy land, connecting homes, gathering spaces, and market stalls. Docks extend over calm pools where [[frogfolk]] dive for freshwater delicacies and children practice their leaps. Every structure is built with both water and land in mind, ensuring that semi-aquatic inhabitants can move freely.

At the heart of Pondside lies a large communal plaza, a shallow-water gathering space where residents meet to discuss village matters, share meals, and play games like [[Bubbleball|bubbleball]]. A massive, ancient willow tree—its gnarled roots half-submerged in water—serves as the village’s spiritual and social center, with [[frogfolk]] elders offering wisdom beneath its shade.
