Floating atop [[Lilyglow Pond]], Lilypad Village is a waterborne settlement made up of interconnected wooden platforms, floating rafts, and houses built atop massive lily pads. [[Frogfolk]] make up most of its population, though other water-loving [[animalfolk]] - like [[otterfolk]] and [[turtlefolk]] - call it home. The village is a serene yet lively place, where homes and shops gently bob on the water, strung together by woven reed bridges and rope walkways.

Many structures in Lilypad Village are made from driftwood, woven reeds, and river stones, blending seamlessly into the natural beauty of the pond. Some homes are built directly into the bases of enormous lily pads, where bioluminescent flowers provide natural light at night. Others rest on stilts, just high enough to keep their doorsteps dry but close enough to let residents dip their feet in the cool waters below.
