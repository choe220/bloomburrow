Nestled beside [[Pondside]], Lilyglow Pond is a tranquil expanse of still, glassy water, its surface dotted with wide lily pads that bloom into bioluminescent flowers at twilight. When the sun dips below the horizon, these lilies glow with soft blue, violet, and silver light, casting shimmering reflections across the water. Fireflies drift lazily over the reeds, their flickering dance mirrored by the tiny fish that dart just beneath the surface. The croak of [[Frogfolk|frogs]] and the distant hum of nocturnal insects blend into a soothing chorus that defines the rhythm of life here.

The pond’s crystal-clear waters are fed by [[Pond Creek]], a meandering stream that tumbles over mossy stones and through dense reeds before emptying into the pond. The water is said to have a faint cooling magic, making it especially refreshing in the desert’s heat. The pond itself is shallow near its banks, but a deep, shadowy center is home to hidden caves and sunken roots, rumored to hold secrets from an older time.

Every year, when the lilies reach their brightest glow, the [[frogfolk]] and other villagers gather at the water’s edge for an all-night festival of music, games, and storytelling called the Nightblooming Festival. Floating lanterns drift across the pond, mimicking the lilies’ glow, and the water is said to carry wishes to unseen spirits.
