The Burning Bog is a smoldering, cursed expanse that marks the boundary between [[Valley]] and the unknown lands beyond, its origins tied to the ancient echoes of the [[Calamity Beasts]]. While the beasts themselves no longer haunt the land, the bog is said to have been born from the aftermath of their great [[War of Cinders]], a place where the land was scorched by their destructive power. Though the beasts have long been defeated, the bog remains a reminder of the devastation they caused - an ever-burning, perpetual fire that refuses to die. Its ground is soft and treacherous, with pools of murky, boiling water that seem to bubble from the earth’s core, while the air is thick with acrid smoke and the scent of sulfur.

The bog’s smoldering state persists without explanation, as if something within the earth refuses to let go of its fiery past. It is a land of rot and ruin, where no plant life thrives except for charred reeds and creeping moss. While the influence of the [[Calamity Beasts]] has faded, their legacy lingers in the land itself, leaving the bog as a boundary that separates the familiar world of [[Valley]] from the mysterious, untamed realms beyond. Few dare to approach it, as the bog is not only inhospitable but is said to be a place where strange visions and unsettling whispers emerge, hinting at the ancient powers that once ravaged the land.
