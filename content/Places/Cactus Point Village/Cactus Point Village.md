Cactus Point is a stubborn settlement built into the shadows of towering saguaro-like cacti, some of which are older than memory itself. It is a village of survivors, wanderers, and those who thrive in the harsh beauty of the desert. At night, glowing desert flowers bloom along the branches, bathing the village in an eerie bioluminescent glow. Not many [[Animalfolk|animalfolk]] call this village their home. The defining landmark of Cactus Point is its forest of massive cacti, known as the [[Spinewood Thicket]]. Hollowed out cores of these massive cacti hold precious water, tended by [[Waterkeepers]]. Cacti grow partially entwined causing natural bridges to span between making it easy to get from place to place above the desert sand. Beneath the harsh desert lies a vast hidden reservoir sunken wellspring. It is accessible via deep tunnels, guarded fiercely by the village. This is where the prosperity comes from, making Cactus Point Village an oasis in an otherwise harsh wasteland. Only trusted villagers are allowed to draw water from the wellspring, and to steal from it is a crime punishable by exile.

The primary inhabitants include gerbilfolk (a subspecies of [[mousefolk]] with long hind legs for hopping across the hot sand, strong digging claws for burrowing, and an ability to conserve water efficiently), [[Lizardfolk|lizardfolk]], and some [[Batfolk|batfolk]]. [[Raccoonfolk]] wander through, seeking relics of long-forgotten desert ruins. Traders stop in to resupply and trade water, tools, and stories before moving on.

# Government
The lord of Cactus Point Village is [[Lord Needle]], a resilient gerbilfolk. They represent the village on the [[Valley Council]].
