The Ironroot Trees are ancient, petrified ash trees that have undergone a unique transformation over millennia. Once living trees, they now stand as towering, metallic giants made entirely of iron. Their trunks, bark, and branches have turned to rusted metal, while their cores contain progressively purer forms of iron as the trees age. The oldest trees, with their thickest trunks, possess cores of near-pure steel, and their branches are sharp and pointed like jagged blades. Despite being petrified, the Ironroot Trees continue to grow, their metal branches reaching upward, with the bark gradually oxidizing into rust over time. These trees have become a vital resource for the inhabitants of [[Valley]], particularly the [[lizardfolk]], who harvest the iron from the trees to craft weapons and armor.

The Ironroot Trees are not only a source of material wealth but are also revered as sacred beings by the [[lizardfolk]], who believe the trees are infused with the strength of their ancestors. The forest they inhabit is a place of pilgrimage, where the [[lizardfolk]] come to honor the trees and the powerful metal they provide. The Ironroot Trees' unique properties make them a symbol of endurance, strength, and the passage of time, with each tree’s metal core serving as a tangible link to the distant past.
