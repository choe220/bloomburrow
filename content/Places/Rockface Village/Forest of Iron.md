The Forest of Iron is a mystical and ancient woodland hidden within a deep fissure, home to the legendary [[Ironroot Tree|Ironroot Trees]] - petrified ash trees made of iron. These trees still grow, their branches sharp and pointed, while their bark oxidizes into rust, creating a striking and eerie landscape. The forest serves as a vital resource for [[Rockface Village]], providing a steady supply of iron for the crafting of weapons and armor. The thicker the trees, the purer the metal at their core, with the oldest trees containing pure steel within their trunks. Each tree’s rings reveal increasing impurities, marking the passage of time and the strength of the iron within. The forest is also a sacred place for the [[lizardfolk]], who view it as a pilgrimage site, believing that the iron of the trees is infused with the strength and spirit of their ancestors.
