Rockface Village is a rugged settlement carved into the sheer side of a cliff, nestled just northwest of the [[Forest of Iron]]. The village is home to a majority of [[lizardfolk]], who have built their homes into the rock itself, using the natural landscape to create sturdy, stone dwellings that blend seamlessly with the cliffside. The village is a testament to the resilience and craftsmanship of its inhabitants, who live in harmony with the harsh, rocky terrain. Its most notable feature is the [[Emberheart Forge]], a legendary smithy where the majority of weapons and armor in [[Valley]] are crafted. The village itself is a testament to the [[lizardfolk]]'s connection to both the land and the powerful forces that shape their culture, with its towering cliffside homes and the steady hum of the forge creating a unique and industrious atmosphere.
