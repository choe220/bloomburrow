Nerys is a [[rabbitfolk]] who exudes a practical, no-nonsense demeanor. She has long ears, a nose that twitches with either humor or irritation and red eyes capable of delivering a sharp, critical glare. She wears a leaf-brimmed hat and has a fluffy tail that she flicks when expressing her disapproval. Nerys is the kind of individual who values hard work, discipline, and living in the present, showing little patience for idle distractions or abstract pursuits.

Nerys is a seasoned resident of [[Pondside]], a village where every creature is expected to contribute meaningfully to the community's well-being. Her straightforward, no-frills approach to life reflects her deep-rooted belief in the importance of hard work and responsibility. Having likely witnessed the struggles and failures of those who indulged in daydreams or idle activities, Nerys has become a vocal advocate for practical living, scolding others for their lack of focus and dedication. Nerys lived a life shaped by the expectations of her community, carrying on the values of previous generations with a firm resolve. Her history is one of dedication to her village and its way of life, fostering a somewhat stern but well-meaning outlook that she shares with those around her
