A rough bunch of [[Animalfolk|animalfolk]]. This group traverses the [[Marrowfen Marsh]] to the [[Calamity Graveyard]] gathering [[Corpseberry|corpseberries]]. They operate out of a building on the docks of the [[Root Market]] in [[Oakhollow Village|Oakhollow]]. Crates and crates of their berries are shipped out of the village and down the [[Long River]] to places like [[The Knowledge Ossuary]] where the weavers and archivists use them for unknown reasons.
