#### Rates
**Common Room Cot** – 3 silver per night (cozy but shared with other travelers)
**Simple Burrow** – 8 silver per night (a snug, earthen room with a straw-stuffed mattress)
**Traveler’s Nook** – 1 gold per night (private, with a sturdy wooden bed and a small writing desk)
#### Food
Breakfast (fresh bread, roasted nuts, and berry preserves) is included with every stay.
