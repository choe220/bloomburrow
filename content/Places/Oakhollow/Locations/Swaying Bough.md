A rustic and welcoming inn at the heart of [[Oakhollow Village|Oakhollow]], built into the trunk of a massive oak. Its walls are adorned with carved wooden reliefs telling old [[Valley]] tales, and a great stone hearth crackles with warmth year-round. Travelers stop in for acorn bread, mushroom stew, and a mug of sweet root ale, while locals gather to trade stories beneath the glow of fireflies in hanging lanterns.
#### Rates
**Common Room Cot** – 3 silver per night (cozy but shared with other travelers)
**Simple Burrow** – 8 silver per night (a snug, earthen room with a straw-stuffed mattress)
**Traveler’s Nook** – 1 gold per night (private, with a sturdy wooden bed and a small writing desk)
#### Food
Breakfast (fresh bread, roasted nuts, and berry preserves) is included with every stay.
