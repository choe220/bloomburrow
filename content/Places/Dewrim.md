Dewrim is a hidden village located along the main road near [[Pondside]] and [[Mintvale]], its presence nearly invisible due to its unique design and the surrounding tall grass. The village is home to a peaceful community of [[Mousefolk]], who have perfected the art of weaving their homes from strands of green grass and sticks. The houses are small, dome-shaped, and woven to blend seamlessly into the tall grass, standing no higher than the surrounding meadow to maintain a sense of unity with nature. Winding paths made from worn earth connect the homes, leading to a communal gathering space in the center, where larger woven structures serve as places for meetings and celebrations. The village’s construction is both practical and artistic, with intricate weaving patterns that highlight the villagers' craftsmanship and respect for the land.

The village’s design emphasizes sustainability and harmony with nature, as Dewrim’s inhabitants only use local materials like grass, reeds, and twigs, ensuring minimal impact on the environment. There are no large farms or deforested areas—just small, well-tended gardens around each home. While Dewrim remains largely secret due to its camouflaged nature, the nearby [[Pondside]] provides the community with a calming backdrop and resources for fishing and gathering reeds. The [[Mousefolk]] take great care to maintain their hidden village, passing down their weaving knowledge to younger generations and trading their finely crafted goods, such as baskets and clothing, with nearby villages.
