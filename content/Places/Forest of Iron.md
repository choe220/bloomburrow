The Forest of Iron is a forest of ancient, petrified ash trees made of iron, ([[Ironroot Tree|Ironroot Trees]]) hidden within a deep fissure. The trees still grow, their branches becoming sharp and pointed while their bark oxidizes into rust. The forest is an important source of iron for armor and weapons. The thicker the tree, the more pure the metal towards the center is, with each "tree ring" containing more and more impurities — the most ancient trees have cores of pure steel. The forest is a site of pilgrimage for [[lizardfolk]].
