Haymeadow is a peaceful and picturesque village located at the southern end of [[Valley]]'s main road, serving as a gateway between the fertile plains of the south and the more rugged regions of [[Valley]]. The village is named for its abundant meadows, which bloom with lush, tall grasses and wildflowers, often swaying in the warm breeze that flows in from the southern hills.

The village is made up of primarily [[mousefolk]] and [[rabbitfolk]], though there are some [[Squirrelfolk|squirrelfolk]] that call it home as well. The buildings, made of stone and timber with thatched roofs, blend harmoniously with the surrounding landscape, their walls adorned with climbing vines and wildflowers. Homes are compact and cozy, often with small gardens that overflow with herbs and vegetables. The central marketplace buzzes with life, filled with wooden stalls draped in vibrant cloth.
