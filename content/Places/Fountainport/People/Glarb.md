Glarb is the [[Frogfolk|frogfolk]] wizard king of [[Fountainport]]. While he holds the traditional title of king, the monarchy was replaced by the [[Valley Council]] and the system of local lords. His role is now that of a respected elder and ceremonial figurehead. He wasn't always the schemer he is now but was once a mentor to those who would study augury. He founded and ran [[The Sunken Lyceum]], a great school for those who wanted to herald the seasons and guide the future of [[Valley]] toward prosperity. Glarb became famous for not only his knowledge, but also his consideration for the cycles and seasons that gave [[Valley]] life. But visions of [[Valley]]'s destruction at the paws and talons of [[Calamity Beasts]] haunted his dreams for many seasons.
