The Sunken Lyceum is an ancient and respected academy of magic located in the submerged lower levels of [[Fountainport]]. Founded by the wizard-king [[Glarb]], the school is dedicated to the study of divination, augury, and the deep connection between the [[animalfolk]] and the natural cycles of [[Valley]].

## Purpose and Curriculum

The primary purpose of The Sunken Lyceum is to train mages and scholars in the arts of foresight and wisdom, preparing them to guide their communities and uphold the balance of the seasons. The curriculum focuses on:
- **Seasonal Augury:** Predicting future events by observing the subtle shifts in nature, the stars, and the flow of the [[Long River]].
- **Scrying and Divination:** Using pools of clear water, reflective crystals, and other magical means to gain insight into the past, present, and future.
- **Hydro-Scrying:** A unique form of divination developed at the Lyceum that uses the ripples and currents of water to interpret omens.
- **History and Lore:** Studying the history of the [[Calamity Beasts]], the [[Omenpath]], and the great cycles that govern [[Valley]].

## Architecture and Location

The Lyceum is a marvel of magical and mundane engineering. Its halls and classrooms are carved from the same soapstone as the rest of [[Fountainport]], but they are submerged beneath the surface of the great pond. Large, magically-treated glass windows look out into the water, allowing the muted light of the pond to filter in, along with the glow of bioluminescent moss and fungi that are cultivated in underwater gardens.

## Current Status

While the Lyceum is not as prominent as it was in its heyday under [[King Glarb|King Glarb's]] direct leadership, it remains an active and important institution in [[Fountainport]]. It is now run by a council of senior augurs, and continues to accept students from all over [[Valley]] who show an aptitude for the divinatory arts.
