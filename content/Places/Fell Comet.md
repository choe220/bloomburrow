A ﬂoating comet above an underground pond deep beneath the surface of [[Valley]]. It is worshiped by [[ratfolk]]. Water leaks down from the caves above, streaming over and around the comet and freezing into a basin of dry, icy land directly below that's used as a proving ground. Young [[ratfolk]] must reach the center of the caverns and chip off a chunk from the icy rock as a test of their cunning and ingenuity.
