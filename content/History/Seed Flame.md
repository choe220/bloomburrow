The Seed Flame, also called the Ember Seed, was a mysterious and primal force that granted immense power over nature itself. It was said to be a spark of pure, radiant energy, capable of turning its bearer into a living sun, brimming with life-giving warmth and destructive fury. The one who wielded the Seed Flame, [[Lily Emberseed|Lily of Valley]], could channel the raw forces of [[Valley]], shaping fire and growth in equal measure, much like the [[Calamity Beasts]] themselves. Its origins remain unknown - some believe it was a fragment of [[Valley]]'s own essence, while others suspect it was drawn from the same chaotic energy that fueled the [[Calamity Beasts|Beasts]]. With its last known wielder lost to history, the Seed Flame has faded into legend. Yet whispers persist that it is not truly gone, only dormant, waiting for the right soul to awaken it once more.
