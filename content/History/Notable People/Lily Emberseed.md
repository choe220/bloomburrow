**Lily Emberseed**, commonly called **Lily of Valley**, was a legendary [[mousefolk]] from [[Valley]].

Lily Emberseed, originally known as Lily of Valley, was a [[mousefolk]] champion of the [[Order of the Holly Leaf]]. Lily was born in the tunnels beneath [[Valley]] during the [[History|#War of Cinders]]. She grew up a humble farmer, pruning the great mushrooms that dotted the tunnels of her birth. When her family was driven to the heart of [[Valley]] and she faced the [[Calamity Beasts|Calamity Beast]] [[Creatures/Wildsear]], the [[Seed Flame]], also called the Ember Seed, ignited in her soul. The Seed Flame turned Lily into a radiant sun, giving her the power of nature like a [[Calamity Beasts|Calamity Beast]] and earning her the moniker "Emberseed." For three days, the light burned and drove the predators back, weakening them as it spread across all of [[Valley]]. In the war's final battle, she fought [[Creatures/Wildsear]], chipping its tooth and sending the beast beyond the [[Brambles]], outside [[Valley]]. Lily fashioned the Wildfire Wolf's tooth into the magical sword [[History/Notable Objects/Cragflame]]. After the war, Lily left [[Valley]] to travel the [[Brambles]], leaving her armor and sword to her descendants.
