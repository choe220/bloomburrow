Omenpaths are rare, shimmering planar portals that sometimes appear in [[Valley]], connecting it to other worlds beyond the [[Brambles]]. These rifts are unpredictable, often opening and closing without warning. Some appear as fleeting flashes of light, while others linger like cracks in reality, their edges glowing with swirling colors. They are said to be fragments of ancient weaves, remnants of powerful magic that once bound the multiverse together.
